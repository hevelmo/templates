Hot looks, cool style

From its bold front grille to the tip of its spoiler, every inch of the Swift Sport’s design is a thrill. It simply sizzles on the outside with 17” alloy wheels, a complete sports body kit, striking dual exhaust and an aggressive front grille.

Like no other

Inside things get even hotter when you slide into the front bucket seats. Cast your eyes over the easy to read 5-meter cluster as the engine roars to life, grip the leather steering wheel and pump the stainless steel pedals.

There’s no other feeling like it – and no other hatch that’s this hot, inside and out.

True sports performance

With a powerful 100kW engine, fierce power-to-weight ratio and the latest performance enhancing technology, the Swift Sport is a true sports compact hatch that embodies Suzuki’s sporty DNA.

A highly charged 1.6L engine gives flawless performance and fuel economy, whether you choose the 6-speed manual or CVT automatic with F1™ inspired paddle shift.

High performance shock absorbers ensure razor-sharp handling and serious road grip. Acceleration is exhilarating; cornering is flat, smooth and controlled. The Swift Sport is built for drivers who love to drive.

Comprehensive safety

While performance takes a front seat in the Swift Sport, it’s reassuring to know the Swift Sport has a 5 star ANCAP safety rating and a comprehensive set of safety features. The special chassis design, wheels and tyres, steering and braking systems work in harmony to ensure excellent grip and totally predictable handling.

Seven airbags combine with ABS, EBD, Electronic Stability Programme (ESP®) and Suzuki’s TECT body construction to give Swift Sport the highest ANCAP safety rating possible.

The tech you expect

Check out the dynamic black cockpit design with silver detailing – you’ll find plenty of impressive features including Bluetooth and iPod connectivity, cruise control, keyless start, 6 speaker CD tuner with MP3 playback, steering-wheel audio controls and digital climate control.

Why stop now? The road is calling.

Powerfully efficient

The Swift Sport is one serious driving machine. Thanks to the specially designed 1600cc VVT petrol engine, twin camshaft and other advanced technologies, this hot hatch delivers excellent fuel efficiency without sacrificing handling and performance.

Maximum Power Output: 100/6,900 kW/rpm Maximum Torque: 160/4,400 Nm/rpm

The Swift Sport boasts premium performance with optimum fuel efficiency of 6.1/100km* and reduced carbon emissions of just 144g/km^.
