UNSTOPPABLE SPIRIT

EXHIBITION DETAILS

Two dramatic sculptures developed by Nino Mustica and Gerry McGovern, inspired by the new Discovery Sport, can be seen on the Riverside Terrace, Southbank Centre, Belvedere Road London, SE1 8XX from 14 – 19 October, 2014.

A UNIQUE COLLABORATION

NINO MUSTICA

Sicilian in origin, but international in scope, artist Nino Mustica has been admired and studied by painters, sculptors and architects. He works from his studio in Milan, Italy.

READ THE ARTICLE

GERRY MCGOVERN

As Design Director and Chief Creative Officer for Land Rover, Gerry McGovern creates some of the world’s most distinctive and desirable vehicles and is recognised as one of the world’s leading automotive designers.

READ THE ARTICLE

THE SCULPTURES

Unstoppable Spirit comprises two sculptures reaching seven metres into the sky. They celebrate the fusion of art and automotive design.
