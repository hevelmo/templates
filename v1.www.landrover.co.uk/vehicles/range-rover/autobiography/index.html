SIGNATURE FEATURES

A FEELING OF LIGHT AND SPACE

Flooding the interior with light, the panoramic roof enhances the feeling of space and a connection to the world outside. The full length power blind provides extra shade when needed.

ENHANCED TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

Offering increased comfort for long journeys, the massage seats feature five programmes and are available for both the driver and front seat passenger.

DISTINCTIVELY AUTOBIOGRAPHY

Autobiography badging on the tailgate, lettering on the tread plates and embossed script on the centre rear squab add the finishing touches.

BEAUTIFUL MATERIALS, FLAWLESS EXECUTION

Contrast-stitched Semi-Aniline leather with high-grade hides is hand-picked for exceptional suppleness and natural appearance.

THE LONG WHEELBASE

Popup content

  • EXCEPTIONAL SPACE AND LUXURY FOR REAR SEAT PASSENGERS

    Range Rover Long Wheelbase has been exceptionally engineered to afford its rear seat passengers generous additional legroom.

  • REAR SEATING OPTIONS

    Supreme travel comfort is enhanced still further with the option of Executive Class Seating. This allows the two rear passengers to recline up to 17 degrees.

  • DEDICATED PASSENGER CONTROLS

    At the touch of a button, rear seat passengers can operate the sliding panoramic sunroof. For extra privacy, activate the powered roof and side door blinds.

  • DISTINGUISHED EMBELLISHMENTS

    Unique Long Wheelbase benefits, adding practicality and exclusivity, include additional stowage space in the door casing and an extended front console.
