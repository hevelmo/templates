1996, DEFENDER

Back to the Birthplace - Road Trip

BY Panda the Landy 2016

Dover, Kent, UK TO Dover, Kent, UK 880 km

After a long term of a 1963 Series 2a Station Wagon and a few overland trips on the African continent with rented and fully kitted Defenders, the decision was finally made to look for a suitable Defender to purchase back home. It was not long until we stumbled across a mint Defender 90 2.0i Station Wagon. Because of the white & black theme it soon became known by its nickname: Panda the Landy.

Panda was born in Solihull in the year 1996 as part of a batch of 840 Lord Blue Defenders, specially built for the Italian Carabinieri. After serving the Italian Carabinieri for many years, Panda was resprayed white to serve United Nations in former Yugoslavia. After retiring from official government duties, Panda was adopted by us.

Because of the fact that 2016 is the year that the last Defender rolled off the production line in Solihull and Panda celebrates its twentieth birthday, we immediately had the idea of a ‘back to the birthplace’ road trip. Via Dunkirk we crossed the Channel to Dover and with a stopover in Oxford we finally reached Solihull. We had a very warm welcome at the Land Rover Experience Centre on the Solihull factory compound. After twenty years Panda was back at his place of birth! Geoff took us on a wonderful three hour tour through the Solihull factory and pointed out the old factory building where Panda rolled off the production line twenty years ago. Thanks Geoff! Can we borrow the Defender 147 for a party anytime soon? Cheers!

After the Land Rover factory tour in Solihull we also visited HUE and the rest of the Land Rover collection at the British Motor Museum at Gaydon & dwelled the beautiful Cotswolds for a couple of days and drove further south to the New Forest National Park. At that point we started to follow the coastline back to Dover, walked the Seven Sisters and finally took the ferry back to Dunkirk and drove back home. It was a memorable trip and we can’t wait for our next adventure with Panda the Landy!
