Suzuki Alto. Choose to have it all.

The Suzuki Alto is the number one choice for value. A vehicle packed with big features. A vehicle with zippy performance. And most importantly, a vehicle primed for an energetic lifestyle.

Which means it has a lot more in common with the Suzuki Night Market than meets the eye. Both are award winners. Both have style and energy in spades. Both are incredibly environmentally conscious. And affordable value means neither will burn a hole in your pocket at the end of the day. A winning combination all round.

So what is the Suzuki Night Market?

Known for its unique mix of world foods, music and wares, the Suzuki Night Market is a Melbourne tradition throughout the balmy summer months and an event Suzuki has been proud to put its name to for seven years.

Since its inception in 1998 it has grown to become one of the premier seasonal events in Melbourne offering a vastly different feel to the traditional day market experience. In fact, last year it was recognised in the Victorian Tourism Awards as the original and largest open-air night market in the southern hemisphere, attracting over 27,000 patrons every single week.

Every Wednesday evening the familiar Queen Victoria Market sheds are transformed into the ultimate carnival-like atmosphere with everything from hawker-style world foods, bars, live bands, regional food, wine tastings, busker-style entertainment and of course local artisans and designers.

Tempt your taste buds from the food stalls offering everything from traditional African curries through to the sweetest of honey dumplings. Treat yourself to a foot massage or palm reading from the Health & Harmony stalls. And at the end of it all, kick back with a beer, wine or sangria and soak up the vibes of the live music and entertainment.  

So whether it’s a night out with the work mates or a catch up with friends, the Suzuki Night Market is the perfect excuse to spend your summer evenings outside. Mark it in your diary.

The Suzuki Night Market. From 16 November 2011 to 29 February 2012, every Wednesday evening from 5.30pm to 10pm.

 

Facts about the Suzuki Night Market:

  • Over 45 food vendors providing a wide range of multicultural cuisines, all prepared and cooked fresh on site;

  • Five fully-licensed bars featuring local-made wines and other beverages;

  • Regional food producers offering delightful tastings and take-home packs;

  • Entertainment for all tastes and ages including live bands, roving performers and children’s activities across two stages;

  • Young designer fashion and artisan stalls with an emphasis on the unique, innovative and bazaar;

  • And a health and harmony area dedicated to mind, body and spirit including massages, reflexology and tarot readings
