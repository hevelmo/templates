ACCESSIBILITY, TOWING & PROTECTION

SAFE AND DEPENDABLE TOWING

Tow balls, tow straps or tow bars, fixed or adjustable, with electrics or without, are all available for demanding loads.

QUICK STEPS TO EASE ENTRY AND EXIT

A range of steps, from spring-loaded folding steps to fixed steps and side runners, provide safe and easy accessibility to the cabin and rear loadspace.

PROTECTION THAT LOOKS THE PART

Choose rugged aluminium Chequer-Plate Protector Kits for your vehicle’s wing tops and/or its sill edge, rear corners and front bumpers.

DEFENCE AGAINST DANGERS UNSEEN

The Under-ride Protection Bar and Ribbed Undershield guard the engine and steering mechanisms while you make your way over rocky ground.
