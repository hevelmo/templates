2015, DEFENDER

Alternative Living: France to Norway

BY Alex Strohl 2015

Les Vans, France TO Reine, Norway 5796 km

Owning a Defender was always a childhood dream. As soon as we got news that it was going to be discontinued, my wife and I began frantically calling Land Rover dealerships throughout France. After a few attempts, we managed to finally get our hands on a 2015 Defender 110. It wasn't easy to contain the excitement.

The obvious choice then, was to take the Rover on her maiden voyage. After a few weeks of planning we came up with an ambitious route. To take three months in the summer of 2015 and drive from the south of France up to the north of Norway, through multiple countries and eventually, across the arctic circle. Our route would take us across some of the most remote reaches of Italy, Slovenia, Austria, Switzerland and Norway. Camping under the starry skies of the Swiss Alps, wading meandering rivers in the backwoods of Slovenia, eating meats and cheeses from Italian farmers in the Dolomites and weathering storms in the Lofoten Islands.
