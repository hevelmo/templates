TECHNOLOGY

  • WADE SENSING™

    Using sensors in the door mirrors when the vehicle is wading, it alerts the driver if the water is too deep. It provides real-time pictorial information, including the maximum wading capability, and is particularly useful in the dark or at night.

  • LAND ROVER INCONTROL™ APPS

    Make life easier with the Smartphone App. Check fuel levels remotely, find your car in busy car parks and receive an SMS alert if you leave anything open. SOS Emergency Call and Optimised Land Rover Assistance use GPS technology to minimise delay in getting assistance to your exact location.

  • INCREASED VISIBILITY

    With five digital cameras mounted around the vehicle, the Surround Camera System provides a near 360° view. This greatly aids low-speed manoeuvrabilty in such situations as parking. All Round Vision, Junction View, Rear View, Tow Assist and Tow Hitch Assist are included in this advanced system.

SAFETY & SECURITY

DRIVE WITH CONFIDENCE

Blind Spot Monitoring with Closing Vehicle Sensing alerts you if a vehicle enters a blind spot or is closing in quickly from behind.

KEYLESS ENTRY

Along with the remote Compact Smart Key, the Keyless Entry system enables you to simply open the door, get in and push the Start button.

REVERSE TRAFFIC DETECTION

Using radar detectors in the rear of the vehicle, the system is designed to warn you about potential collisions during reversing manoeuvres.
