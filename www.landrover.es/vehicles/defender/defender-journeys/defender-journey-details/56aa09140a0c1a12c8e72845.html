2003, DEFENDER

G4 Challenge - South Africa

BY G4 by Land Rover 2003

Cape Town, South Africa TO Eden St, Kimberley, 8300, South Africa 953 km

In this international adventure race, 16 teams used Land Rover Defenders to reach extreme locations in the Great Karoo Desert and Lesotho Mountains in South Africa. Competitors performed outdoor extreme sport challenges at each stop, battling to win the prize of a brand new Range Rover. Belgian fighter pilot Rudi Thoelen eventually won but declined the prize. He had been so impressed with the Defenders' performance he asked for two Defenders instead.
