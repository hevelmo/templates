    • Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)

    • Semi-Aniline Leather Seat ‘Style 24‘ (18-way drivers/18-way passenger front seats, front and rear climate seats, front massage seats with adjustable seat bolsters , driver and passenger memory, rear bench seat with load through facility and rear power recline, four way rear seat power lumbar and passenger seat away)
    • Driver and passenger front seat armrests

    • Active Speed Limiter (ASL)

    • Perimetric alarm with engine immobilisation
    • Power operated child locks
