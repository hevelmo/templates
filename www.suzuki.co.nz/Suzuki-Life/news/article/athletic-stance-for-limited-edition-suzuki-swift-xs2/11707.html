Athletic Stance For Limited Edition Suzuki Swift XS2

The new Swift XS2 incorporates distinctive body features that add individuality to the evergreen five-door hatchback.

HRS aluminium alloy wheels provide a more athletic profile and make the Swift stand out in a crowd.

An XS2 graphic and a neat spoiler fitted to the top of the hatchback door also distinguish the latest version of the top-selling Swift.

The compact Suzuki has remote control door locking, 60/40 split folding rear seats, air conditioning, a height adjustable driverâ€™s seat, tilt adjustable steering and electrically operated windows and door mirrors. A Sony audio system and four speakers are also standard.

The XS2 has UV cut front door glass and a computer display for fuel consumption and fuel range distance to empty.

An economical, low emission 1.4-litre double overhead camshaft 4-cylinder engine with variable valve technology produces 70kW of power at 6,000 rpm.
