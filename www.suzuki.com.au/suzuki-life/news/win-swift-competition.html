Win a Swift competition

Updated 6/7/2011

Suzuki has launched an online competition whereby one lucky fan will win the keys to their very own brand new Suzuki Swift. The crux of the competition? Simple - be our friend on Facebook! 

 

Just click on the link above and 'like' our page and you will be in the draw. And don't forget to tell all your friends!

 

How to enter:

Entrants must first register for a Facebook account. Once registered, visit the Suzuki Australia Facebook page (www.facebook.com/suzukiaustralia) and ‘like’ the page. Then click on the “Win a Suzuki Swift” competition tab. In the competition form provided, complete all fields, ensuring you have entered your full name, address, email and daytime telephone number.
