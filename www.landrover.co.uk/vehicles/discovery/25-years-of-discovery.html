25 YEARS OF DISCOVERY

Peter and Eileen Crichton have dedicated much of the last 25 years to exploring the world in Rabia, their Discovery 3.5V8i. They’ve circumnavigated the globe twice along the Northern and Southern hemisphere, and in 2012, travelled across the lines of latitude from the most northerly to most southerly points accessible by a 4x4.

YOUR STORIES

  • DESERT CROSSING

    'About three years ago I picked up a very early Discovery. Recently a friend and I took off on a massive trip across Australia (6000 Km) with the main intention of crossing the Simpson Desert. We have now returned and the car didn't miss a beat.'

    MIKE MONTGOMERY, AUSTRALIA

  • SEARCH AND RESCUE

    ‘I joined the Texas Rovers club and learned what my Discovery is capable of from a former Land Rover Experience Instructor. I’ve become part of a non-profit expedition, participated in search and rescue due to floods and tornadoes, and made friends from all over the world I'd never have met without my Discovery.’

    CHRIS DUKE, USA

  • FROM GERMANY TO INDONESIA

    'My Discovery used to be a Federal Police vehicle in Germany with some extra equipment, but it is now my workhorse in Indonesia for weekdays and also my weekend warrior for getaways from camping to overlanding.'

    AHMAD RAVI, INDONESIA

  • TESTING THE CAPABILITY

    'Wonderful holiday with my Discovery in Wales. I very much enjoyed what the car can do in such a special environment.'

    CLEMENTINE BERGMANS, BELGIUM

  • GET THE FAMILY TO THE BEACH

    'I took the family in our Discovery, which we call Diva, on holiday to Denmark's West Coast. The beach is free for cars for many kilometres. You drive on to it directly from the road. My son is setting out to surf.'

    STEFAN MURAWSKI, GERMANY

  • LAND ROVER'S SPIRITUAL HOME

    'My Discovery emerging from the darkness at the Land Rover Experience site at Eastnor.'

    STEPHEN HAYNES, UK

  • REST AND RELAXATION DISCOVERY STYLE

    'I leave work behind and have fun with my Land Rover Discovery.'

    EDUARD CARRILLO, SPAIN

  • INTO THE WOODS

    'Discovery photographed in pine forests in the Altai Region, above the River Ob.'

    OLEG SHUPIN, RUSSIA

  • DISCOVERY IS FOR LIFE

    'My first car was a second-hand 1994 Discovery 200 Tdi. In 1998 I bought a new Discovery 300 TDi that I still drive with great satisfaction. Twenty years of Discovery! The family owns a Range Rover Sport. I am looking forward to the new Discovery, but one thing is certain, I will keep my 300 TDI for life.'

    MATTEO FAROLDI, ITALY
