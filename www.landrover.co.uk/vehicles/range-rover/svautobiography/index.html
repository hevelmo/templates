UNRIVALLED PERFORMANCE

INTERIOR STYLING

INTERIOR STYLING

BRITISH CRAFTSMANSHIP

The interior of SVAutobiography is the epitome of elegant and indulgent living. Only the very finest perforated Semi-Aniline leathers are featured on the seats. Soft and supple, they are sourced from one tannery that uses an organic process featuring bark extract from Black Wattle trees. Sustainable woods and veneers on the centre console and fascia add to the sense of craft and refinement. Our British craftsmen have created an interior that exudes sophistication, finesse and calm.

INTERIOR STYLING

INTERIOR STYLING

REAR EXECUTIVE CLASS SEATING

The rear cabin is beautifully appointed throughout; the executive seat console includes a bottle chiller compartment, power deployable tables, multi-media and climate solutions. Massage seats and power recline of up to 17 degrees are further enhanced on Long Wheelbase by a calf rest, while stowage facilities come readily to hand and deep twist pile mohair carpet mats complete the feeling of sumptuous luxury. The individual seats are separated by an elegant console that houses all the controls. A leather-covered handbook and polished Autobiography key fob with knurled finish add the finishing touches.
