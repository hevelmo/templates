Michael Roderick's Defender Journey

BY Michael Roderick 2016

Canberra ACT, Australia TO Bowen QLD, Australia 4514 km

Two months leave and a new’ish (2015) Defender. Adventure calls. I drove from Canberra to my original home in North Queensland. What a trip. The highlights were the spectacular arid landscapes of western Queensland, fishing on the Burdekin River with mates, and time spent with my wife at one of the fabulous beaches near Bowen.
