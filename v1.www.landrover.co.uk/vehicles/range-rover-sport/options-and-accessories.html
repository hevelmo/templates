INTERIOR OPTIONS

A MASSAGE ON THE MOVE

For total driver and passenger comfort, the Oxford leather 20-way powered front seats can memorise your preferred settings and offer five massage programmes.

COLD CLIMATE CONVENIENCE

Make driving in cold and freezing conditions more comfortable with the heated leather steering wheel.

PERSONAL CLIMATE CONTROL

The 4-Zone Climate Control system provides separate controls for the driver, front passenger and the passengers on each side of the second row of seats.

COMFORT IN ALL CONDITIONS

Choose Climate Front and Rear Seats to manage weather extremes, with cooling technology for hot, sticky days and cosy warmth for frosty mornings.

ADDED CAPABILITY OFF-ROAD

The Off-Road Pack takes the vehicle’s off-road capability to another level, and includes Terrain Response® 2, a Twin Speed Transfer Box and Adaptive Dynamics.

TECHNOLOGY

LANE DEPARTURE AND SPEED LIMIT ALERTS

Surround Camera System displays upcoming speed limits and Lane Departure Warning vibrates the steering wheel to warn you if you drift lanes without indicating.

A CLEAR VIEW ALL ROUND

The Surround Camera System uses five digital cameras discretely placed around the vehicle to provide an almost complete 360 degree view on the Touch Screen.

MAKE LIFE EASIER WITH OUR SMARTPHONE APP

The InControl™ Remote Smartphone App lets you check fuel levels remotely, find your car in busy car parks, see if doors or windows have been left open, and can summon emergency assistance.

PARKING MADE SIMPLE

From choosing a space to manoeuvring in and out accurately, Parallel Parking, Parking Exit and Perpendicular Parking make parking in tight spots easier than ever.

INDULGE IN THE SOUND OF MERIDIAN™

Meridian™ audio technology gives you stunning sound quality. The top of the range system features Meridian™ signature reference Audio with 23 speakers to expand the sense of space in the cabin and give a realistic three-dimensional listening experience. (Meridian is a registered trademark of Meridian Audio Ltd.)

KEEP YOUR PASSENGERS ENTERTAINED

The Rear Entertainment System provides entertainment for the rear passengers with a choice of 8 inch or 10.2 inch video screens and a Digital Audio Broadcast radio. While with the Dual View Screen, the driver and front passenger can view completely different content on the Touch Screen display.

SECURITY

FIND YOUR VEHICLE IF IT GOES MISSING

Land Rover InControl™ Secure will alert you of an attempted theft and signals your vehicle’s location to the Land Rover tracking centre to aid swift recovery.

REMOTE UNLOCKING

Keyless entry enables you to enter the vehicle quickly and safely without having to fumble for keys in your bag or pocket.

SOFT DOOR CLOSING

There’s no need to slam your door shut. Soft Door Close makes sure your door is closed securely, but without the jarring noise.

DESIGNED TO SEE WHAT YOU MIGHT MISS

Blind Spot Monitor with Closing Vehicle Sensing and Reverse Traffic Detection alert you if a vehicle enters a blind spot or is closing quickly from behind.
