New 3-Door Swift Sport Launching in New Zealand

Suzuki will soon add a new dimension to the Swift hatchback, the nationâ€™s long time best-selling small car.

From late April, Suzuki New Zealand will offer the popular 1.6-litre Swift Sport in two body configurations with the arrival of a 3-door version.

The European sourced Swift Sport is additional to the Suzuki range and is similarly specified to the 5-door version which launched last year and has become a top-selling model.

Carrying an impressive five star Euro NCAP safety rating, the Swift Sport 3-door is priced at $25,990, a $1,510 advantage over the 5-door version.

As a car likely to appeal to sports-minded drivers, this latest Swift has its own unique body style and is available only with the 6-speed manual transmission. Suzuki New Zealand is also offering a leather upholstery option which brings the total on-road recommended retail price to $27,500.

In addition to the high level of standard equipment, the 3-door includes several features not found on the 5-door model. These include automatic headlamp operation, a rear fog lamp, tinted rear privacy glass and heated door mirrors with an electric fold function.

At 1,045 kg, the 3-door is slightly lighter than its 5-door brother, is marginally more economical and produces lower emissions.

In the combined European fuel test cycle, the 3-door Swift Sport averages 6.4 litres/100 km (44.1 miles per gallon), and a frugal 5.15 litres/100 km (54.3 mpg) in the extra-urban open road cycle.

The engine is noted for its smoothness and flexibility at low revs, while there is a marked power set-up once the power unit reaches 4,400 rpm when peak torque is produced.

There are four wheel disc brakes, ventilated up front, and the 195/45R17 tyres are fitted to 15-spoke aluminium alloy wheels.

The performance flagship of the Swift range includes as standard a keyless push-button start, cruise control, climate controlled air conditioning, sports seats and Bluetooth hands-free phone connection.
