Practical, convenient with plenty of style

Want style

Elegant and dynamic, the curves of the Baleno flow gracefully from the striking front grille to the chrome trim of the rear hatch. You’ll feel wrapped in luxury from outside in with the Baleno’s sleek and sophisticated lines.

Need space

With Suzuki’s unparalleled small car know-how, every centimeter of the Baleno has been used to its fullest potential. You would never imagine how spacious the interior is given the Baleno's exterior size.

Ergonomically widened front seats, a high ceiling and generous leg room in the back, let you and your passengers get in and out easily and simply relax.

Need more space

You would be hard-pressed to find a bigger boot in this class. With 355 litres at your disposal, there’s plenty of room for groceries, boxes, golf clubs, sports gear and those bulky purchases from the garden centre.

Fold the 60:40 split folding seats down to more than double that amount and make room for fishing rods, boxes, furniture or really big items.

Want simplicity

Slip into the driver's seat and feel completely comfortable and familiar. The highly visible display unit at the centre of the instrument cluster gives a clear picture of the Baleno’s running performance.

The instrument panel features stylish curved lines and silver accents, housed in a smooth and wide dash. Steering wheel mounted controls for audio, phone and cruise are at your fingertips.

Need technology

Inside, the Baleno is packed with the kind of high-tech and high-spec extras you would expect in a luxury vehicle. The centrally located 7-inch touchscreen is user-friendly with a built-in satellite navigation system and easy to follow maps.

A reversing camera makes parking a breeze and the speed limiter helps you save fuel and drive safely within the limit avoiding unexpected tickets.

Use voice recognition to map out a route, call your friends or enjoy your favourite soundtrack using Bluetooth® and USB port.

Want performance

The Baleno represents a whole new way of thinking. Light and powerful, the Baleno is a joy to drive with great all-round visibility, a tight turning circle and outstanding manoeuvrability.

It’s 4 cylinder 1.4L VVT engine delivers ample power for a zippy feel around town with power in reserve for open road driving, overtaking and hills. Choose from either a 5-speed manual transmission or a smooth electronic 4-stage auto.

Need economy

A lighter car means better handling and improved fuel efficiency or the economy you want and the performance you need. The combined effect is impressive. The Baleno is agile, responsive, and frugal on fuel with a combined consumption of just 5.1-5.4 litres per 100 kilometres.

  • Baleno 1.4 Manual 5.1L/100km*
  • Baleno 1.4 Auto 5.4L/100km*

View the Fuel Economy Calculator

*ADR 81/02 results for combined cycle. Fuel consumption will vary due to factors such as vehicle condition, driving style and traffic conditions.

Want sophistication

The LED daylight running lamps located in the front bumper not only look good, they also increase the Baleno’s visibility – especially from a distance, reducing the likelihood of an accident.

The Guide Me light function keeps the headlights illuminated for a short while after you get out and when the doors are unlocked with the remote control, the low beams and position lights turn on to lead you to your car.

Need safety

The Baleno is equipped with a comprehensive range of integrated safety features including;

  • dual, front, side and curtain airbags,
  • Electronic Stability Programme (ESP®),
  • Suzuki’s unique Total Effective Control Technology,
  • ABS with Electronic Brake Distribution, and
  • Brake Assist.

To further enhance visibility and safety, included are a reversing camera with guidelines, LED daytime running lamps and fog lamps (LTD).
