EXPLORAR MÁS

2012, DEFENDER

Pushing the Limits Expedition

BY RGS and Land Rover 2012

London, UK TO Panteleimonivs'ka Street, 25, Odessa, Odessa Oblast, Ukraine, 65000 3613 km

The Land Rover 'Go Beyond' Bursary, in partnership with the Royal Geographical Society (with IBG), was awarded in 2012 to 'Pushing the Limits', a team supporting a disabled adventurer's plans to encourage our understanding of the geography on our doorstep and show that exploration is within everyone's reach, whatever their ability.

As part of the bursary, the team was provided with a Land Rover Defender 110 to enable them to experience places normally out of bounds to wheelchair users. Expedition leader Andy Campbell, was paralysed during a climbing accident in 2004 whilst abseiling from the top of a climb in the Peak District. He said, before embarking on the 'Pushing the Limits' expedition "Life's an adventure that needs to be explored, being in a wheelchair just means you have to be a bit more ingenious - and stubborn - to get where you want."
