It’s just the right amount of Land Rover – spacious and flexible enough to carry what you need and with all the capability you would expect.

TACKLE THE TOUGHEST TERRAINS

It is reassuring to know that Freelander 2 has proved itself at more severe temperatures and in more extreme conditions than you are ever likely to experience.

TAKE THE ROUGH WITH THE SMOOTH

Freelander 2 comes with a host of stability control systems to enhance traction by reducing engine torque and applying a unique braking force to each wheel.

SPACIOUS AND ADAPTABLE

The loadspace is as generous as it is versatile. From 755 to 1,670 litres depending on seat configuration, it’s large enough to take you and whatever you need.

PROTECTION FOR ALL

Enjoy a safer journey thanks to Roll Stability Control and 7 airbags. In Euro NCAP tests, Freelander was awarded a 5-star rating for Adult Occupant Protection.

CAPABILITY

CAPABILITY

READY FOR ANYTHING

The Freelander 2 has been tested beyond the limitations of most. It has performed in temperatures ranging from -40º to +50º. It has been driven at altitudes of up to 14,000 feet. And it benefits from excellent approach and departure angles, coupled with generous ground clearance to tackle the toughest of terrains.

BORN TO PERFORM

All-terrain technologies featured in the Freelander 2 include Hill Descent Control* that takes the strain out of negotiating those difficult slopes and the renowned Terrain Response®* which automatically optimises traction and driveability by adapting to the demands of the terrain.

BOOK AN EXPERIENCE DAY

PUTTING YOU IN CONTROL

You’re ready to take the rough with the smooth, with Freelander 2’s stability control systems. They work together to reduce speed and apply braking force to individual wheels, providing support when you most need it.

TOWING POWER

With Freelander 2 you can tow up to 2,000kg. Hitch up your trailer using the rear view camera, and stay safer on the road with Trailer Stability Assist, all with a sector-leading nose weight for towing of 150kg and flexible loadspace.

CARRYING & TOWING ACCESSORIES

A CHOICE OF ENGINES

Quieter, cleaner and powerful even at low speeds, Freelander 2’s 2.2-litre turbocharged diesel engines are available at power outputs of 150hp and 190hp.

  • ALL TERRAIN EXPERTISE

    On tarmac, gravel, sand or snow, Terrain Response® makes sure your drive is responsive, steady and controlled.

  • REDUCED EMISSIONS

    When stopping in manual, Stop/Start turns off the engine, restarting automatically. Intelligent Charging tops up the battery when slowing.

  • STABLE ON STEEP HILLS

    For steady hill descent in rough terrain you don’t need to brake as Gradient Release and Hill Descent Controls take you smoothly down.

  • MUSIC AS IT WAS INTENDED

    Give your journey the soundtrack it deserves with industry-leading Meridian™* surround sound. (Meridian is a registered trademark of Meridian Audio Ltd.) *Only available on SE Tech and Metropolis derivatives.

  • ELECTRONIC PARKING BRAKE

    It’s easy to apply the parking brake, just switch it on to apply and press down to release, or it automatically releases as you move off.

DESIGN

A glance at the vehicle’s purposeful stance and contemporary looks tells you its design cues are of the moment. The Freelander 2’s sleek front and rear lamps, front grille and vent finishers add greatly to the sense of modernity and muscularity.
