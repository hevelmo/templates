Suzuki Baleno Everything you want,
everything you need.

Elegant and dynamic, the new Baleno combines style with space. The interior is unbelievably roomy and the boot is practically enormous. With a powerfully efficient 1.4 litre engine the Baleno offers you the economy you want and the performance you need. Plus you get all the latest mod-cons and safety features – for a lot less than you would expect.

The Baleno’s next-generation frame has freed up an enormous amount of space in the cabin. With Suzuki’s unparalleled small car know-how, every centimeter has been used to its fullest potential. Sitting inside, the sense of openness and freedom is almost unbelievable – especially when you consider the Baleno’s compact exterior.

From your very first drive the Baleno will feel comfortable and familiar. The centrally located 7-inch touchscreen is user-friendly with a built-in satellite navigation system and easy to follow maps. A reversing camera makes parking a breeze and there’s Bluetooth® and USB so you can answer calls or change tracks without taking your hands off the wheel. The Baleno also offers Apple CarPlay so you can use apps on the touchscreen.

Comfort and Space

All the extra room means it’s easy to relax in the Baleno’s quiet, well-appointed interior. The ergonomically designed front seats have been widened for more shoulder room and a wider rear seat means even the longest journeys will be fidget free. The taller members of your team can stretch out and the Baleno’s high ceiling makes it a breeze to get in and out.

You would be hard-pressed to find a bigger boot in this class. With 355 L of space at your disposal, there’s plenty of room for groceries, boxes, golf clubs, sports gear and those bulky purchases from the garden centre. 60/40 split seats add even more flexibility. Fold one down to make room for fishing rods and other long items, or fold them both flat to take on piles of boxes, furniture or really big items.

Economy and Performance

Thanks to its advanced weight-saving frame the Baleno is agile, responsive, and frugal on fuel with a combined consumption of just 5.1 litres/100km (GLX manual combined cycle). A 4 cylinder 1.4L VVT engine delivers ample power for a zippy feel around town with power in reserve for open road driving, overtaking and hills. This is paired to either 5-speed manual transmission or a smooth electronic 4-stage auto.

Comprehensive safety systems include 6 airbags, ABS and ESP.
