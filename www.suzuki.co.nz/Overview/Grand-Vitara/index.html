No road too tough

The Grand Vitara’s 4-mode 4×4 system transmits power to all four wheels at all times, ensuring positive traction regardless of the road surface. A switch built into the centre console lets you quickly and easily change modes between;

  • 4H for most driving conditions,
  • 4H LOCK for serious off-roading,
  • 4L LOCK for low-range traction on extreme surface conditions, and
  • N (neutral) mode, specifically intended for motor home owners wishing to tow along an SUV to use as a runabout.*

* Refer to owner’s manual for full instructions on recreational towing.

Built for the great escape

Whether it’s a trip to the snow, the beach, or camping with the family, you can rely on the Grand Vitara to get you there in comfort and style.

The Grand Vitara’s tough ladder frame is integrated with a monocoque body to increase rigidity and durability without adding weight. Its high ground clearance, single frame chassis and powerful engine make it ideal for towing.

Fully independent suspension with front MacPherson struts and rear multi-link geometry reduce body noise, for smoother on-road cruising and impressive off-road traction. The Grand Vitara gives you freedom to go where you want and the stability and quietness to enjoy every minute of it.

Rugged good looks

The Grand Vitara is as much at home on a city street as it is off the beaten track. Combining amazing space and practicality, the Grand Vitara is a stand-out with its rugged SUV design, striking front grille, silver roof rails, sunroof (LTD) and 18 inch alloy wheels (LTD).

The fully covered spare wheel is mounted on the vertically hinged luggage door in traditional 4WD style, and the elegant chrome side mouldings (LTD) add the finishing touch.

Loads of space

The Grand Vitara’s cabin is just as smart as its exterior. Friends and family can stretch out with plenty of leg and head room. With lots of clever pockets, and holders for water bottles, sunglasses and other odds and ends, there’s no need for clutter – everything has a place.

Loads of flexibility

The Grand Vitara's split rear seats mean each passenger can recline separately. Or you can fold the seats flat for a massive 758 litres of boot space – perfect for fishing rods, camping gear and odd-shaped items.

For added security there’s a lockable glovebox and an underfloor storage compartment for items you want to hide from view.

Comfortable and connected

Cruising in the Grand Vitara, all the creature comforts are at your fingertips including:

  • multimedia satellite navigation system with 6.1” touchscreen,
  • hands-free Bluetooth® connectivity,
  • full iPod® integration with USB input,
  • leather steering wheel with mounted audio controls,
  • keyless entry & push-button start (LTD),
  • digital climate control air conditioning, and
  • cruise control.

Safety reassured

You don’t have to go far to find adventure in New Zealand. With the Grand Vitara you can make the most of our country’s rugged beauty in complete confidence. Key safety features standard across the range include 6 airbags - dual front, side and curtain, Electronic Stability Programme (ESP®), ABS and EBD (a system that transfers brake force between axles).

Legendary power & performance

With its powerful 2.4 litre engine, Grand Vitara can take you just about anywhere. Featuring variable valve timing (VVT) technology for increased power, it’s quiet and comfortable on the open road and has plenty of torque for towing. Off the beaten track there’s extra muscle on tap to help you conquer whatever the terrain throws at you. No wonder 4×4 enthusiasts sing Grand Vitara’s praises.

For a smooth, quiet ride and effort-free operation, there’s a 4-speed automatic transmission designed with a sporty shift-gate configuration. Or choose the five-speed manual transmission for complete control, increased fuel economy and sporty performance.
