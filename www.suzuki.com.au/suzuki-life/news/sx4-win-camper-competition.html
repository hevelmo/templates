SX4 Win a Camper Competition

Updated 30/6/2011

Suzuki has teamed up with local camping manufacturer Little Guy Australia to offer SX4 buyers the chance to win a luxury compact camper trailer valued at $14,000.

Anyone who purchases a new SX4 between July 1 and Sept 30 at a participating Suzuki dealership (excluding Queensland) is automatically entered into the draw to win.

 

How to enter:

Simply purchase and have delivered any brand new SX4 vehicle from any participating Suzuki dealership between 1 July 2011 and 30 September 2011 an you'll automatically go into the draw to win a Little Guy Deluxe Plus camper van and accessories valued at over $14,000.

HOW TO ENTER:

To be eligible to enter, simply purchase & have delivered a brand new Suzuki SX4 motor vehicle from a participating Suzuki dealership during the promotional period. 

Promotion ends 5.00pm 30/09/11.
