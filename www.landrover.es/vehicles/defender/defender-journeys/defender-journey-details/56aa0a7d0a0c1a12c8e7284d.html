EXPLORAR MÁS

2013, DEFENDER

HMS Defender

BY Royal Navy by Land Rover 2013

Portsmouth, Portsmouth, UK TO Glasgow, Glasgow City, UK 716 km

Sailors from the Royal Navy's new destroyer, HMS Defender, used Land Rover Defender 110s to support their arduous adventure to raise thousands of pounds for charities. The sailors tackled the notorious Three Peak Challenge: racing between England, Wales and Scotland to climb down Snowdon, Scafell Pike and Ben Nevis in 25 hours. Land Rover has provided the Royal Navy ship with its own Defender to use whenever it needs all-terrain transport on dry land.
