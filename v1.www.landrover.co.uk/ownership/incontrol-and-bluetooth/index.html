LAND ROVER InControl

A range of features that keep you
connected to your vehicle

  • FIND A RETAILER
  • DOWNLOAD A BROCHURE
  • CONTACT US

InControl REMOTE

Check your fuel levels remotely, find your vehicle in a busy car park and even check that you haven’t left a window open – all through a handy smartphone app.

InControl SECURE

InControl Secure uses tracking technology to alert you to an attempted theft, and signals your vehicle's location to police, for a timely recovery.

InControl APPS

Land Rover InControl Apps enables you to use a variety of smartphone apps on your vehicle's touch-screen by connecting your device to the InControl Apps USB port.

InControl Wi-Fi

This provides in-car access to a 3G hotspot, enabling up to eight devices to be used simultaneously. Passengers can work or be entertained throughout the journey.

InControl REMOTE FEATURES

InControl Remote consists of three features: Remote Smartphone App, SOS Emergency Call and Optimised Land Rover Assistance.

Popup content

  • REMOTE SMARTPHONE APP

    The Land Rover InControl Remote Smartphone App lets you prepare for a trip by checking fuel levels and range remotely. You can find your vehicle in a crowded car park or check that you locked the doors and closed the windows. Download the app now – it’s free and includes a demonstration mode.

    DOWNLOAD iPHONE APP DOWNLOAD ANDROID APP

  • SOS EMERGENCY CALL

    In a critical situation, Land Rover’s Emergency Call will bring the emergency services to your exact location. If you are involved in an accident in which the vehicle's airbags deploy you will automatically be put in touch with professional help. Alternatively you can press the SOS Emergency Call button in the car to ask for assistance.

    FIND OUT HOW SOS EMERGENCY CALL WORKS

  • OPTIMISED LAND ROVER ASSISTANCE

    In the unlikely event of your vehicle breaking down, pressing the InControl Optimised Land Rover Assistance button will use satellite positioning and vehicle health information to guide the recovery service to your location, minimising delay.

    FIND OUT HOW OPTIMISED LAND ROVER ASSISTANCE WORKS

BLUETOOTH®

Bluetooth®

Land Rover offers a mobile phone system to which you can connect an approved mobile phone handset and access many of its important functions via the vehicle keypads, voice activation and steering wheel switches.

Which handsets and software versions?

Land Rover engineers have tested and recommended the following handsets to meet our quality standards. Please note that not all Bluetooth® enabled mobile phone handsets are compatible with the Land Rover Bluetooth® Connectivity. This list will be updated as more handsets are tested and new handsets are released.

Ways to connect

The Land Rover Bluetooth® Connectivity is Bluetooth® enabled. This allows the user the freedom to leave their mobile phone handset in a briefcase, or a pocket and make and receive calls.

Bluetooth® Connectivity Centre

The Bluetooth® enabled telephone system is controlled from the steering wheel and from the Touch-screen. It allows the driver to make and receive calls safely and easily using a Bluetooth® compatible mobile phone handset without the need for wires or cradles. Different features will be available according to the mobile phone handset used. This area of the website is designed to help you get the best from the Bluetooth.
