Central Kalahari 2015

BY Gerhard Steenkamp 2015

Bloemfontein, South Africa TO Kweneng West, Botswana 1270 km

During June 2015 we took a trip to Botswana.

Visited Khumaga Game reserve next to the Boteti River. Then went down to Piper Pan in the Central Kalahari Game reserve.

Then went up to Nxai Pans before we travelled back to South Africa after two weeks in Botswana.
