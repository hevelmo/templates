INNOVATION

  • A SMOOTH AND SURE RIDE

    Adjustable air suspension gives a more comfortable ride and can be raised to tackle difficult terrain or lowered for easier loading.

  •
    WADE IN

    Don’t let water get in your way. Sensors in the door mirrors let you know when water levels are near the 700mm maximum wading depth.

  •
    PARK WITH CONFIDENCE

    Get in and out of tight parking spots with Discovery's parking aids, in conjunction with the optional rear view camera.

  •
    AUTOMATIC STOP/START

    To reduce CO2 emissions and save fuel the engine turns off when Discovery stops and restarts when the brake pedal is released.

  •
    SURROUND CAMERA SYSTEM

    Discovery’s five optional digital cameras provide a near 360° view, giving you the confidence to tackle almost any situation.

  •
    STABILISE YOUR TRAILER

    To improve safety when towing Trailer Stability Assist detects sway and corrects by adjusting brakes and throttle.

  •
    SAFER JOURNEYS

    The Exterior Detection Pack includes Blind Spot Monitoring and Closing Vehicle Sensing, which are designed to detect what you might miss so you can overtake or change lanes safely.

  •
    WIRED FOR SOUND

    Enjoy music as it was intended through the optional 11-speaker 380w Audio System or premium 17-speaker 825w Surround Sound System.

  •
    TIMED CLIMATE

    Get conditions just right. Pre-heat the cabin or introduce fresh air with the optional Timed Climate, so it’s ready for when you get in.

SPECIFICATIONS AT A GLANCE

FUEL EFFICIENCY

36.7mpg

The 3-litre, turbocharged and intercooled SDV6 Diesel is coupled with 8-speed automatic transmission and Intelligent Stop/Start technology.

LUGGAGE SPACE

2,558litres

With second and third row seats stowed in the seven seat option. Whether it’s a weekend away or the trip of a lifetime, Discovery has plenty of room.

TOWING

3,500kg

Discovery’s impressive towing capacity is supported by optional Trailer Stability Assist to keep vehicle and trailer under control.

OBSTACLE CLEARANCE

310mm

Off-road height with Air Suspension and manual lift function. There’s room to manoeuvre on even the toughest terrains.
