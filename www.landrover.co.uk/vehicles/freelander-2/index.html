Ever since its inception some 17 years ago, Land Rover Freelander has been on an adventure. The first compact SUV to come off our production lines, its lively nature and ability to tackle any terrain in any conditions made it a firm favourite around the globe. Now, however, the adventure has come to an end as production of the vehicle ended in December 2014.

However, with our Approved Programme you can still purchase a Land Rover Freelander, ensuring it has received the very best in maintenance as this covers a 165 Multi-Point Inspection and is backed by a Two Year Warranty. Naturally a full service programme is also available through your nearest Land Rover Retailer. And should you need any accessories for your vehicle, they will be able to assist with those requirements as well as a comprehensive range of items that will still be available.

Finally, you may be interested to learn about Land Rover New Discovery Sport, our most versatile compact SUV that’s ready for anything. Featuring 9-speed automatic transmission, outstanding fuel economy, class-leading technologies such as Terrain Response® and 5+2 stadium seating, its spirited personality and versatility are matched only by its willingness to perform anywhere, no matter what the conditions. And as the vehicle has already been awarded a EuroNCAP 5 Star Rating, its safety qualities are also without question.

CAPABILITY

CAPABILITY

READY FOR ANYTHING

The Freelander has been tested beyond the limitations of most. It has performed in temperatures ranging from -40º to +50º. It has been driven at altitudes of up to 14,000 feet. And it benefits from excellent approach and departure angles, coupled with generous ground clearance to tackle the toughest of terrains.

APPROVED USED

BORN TO PERFORM

All-terrain technologies featured in the Freelander include Hill Descent Control* that takes the strain out of negotiating those difficult slopes and the renowned Terrain Response®* which automatically optimises traction and driveability by adapting to the demands of the terrain.

EXPLORE ACCESSORIES

PUTTING YOU IN CONTROL

You’re ready to take the rough with the smooth, with Freelander stability control systems. They work together to reduce speed and apply braking force to individual wheels, providing support when you most need it.

APPROVED USED

TOWING POWER

With Freelander you can tow up to 2,000kg. Hitch up your trailer using the rear view camera, and stay safer on the road with Trailer Stability Assist, all with a sector-leading nose weight for towing of 150kg and flexible loadspace.

CARRYING & TOWING ACCESSORIES

A CHOICE OF ENGINES

Quieter, cleaner and powerful even at low speeds, Freelander's 2.2-litre turbocharged diesel engines are available at power outputs of 150hp and 190hp.
