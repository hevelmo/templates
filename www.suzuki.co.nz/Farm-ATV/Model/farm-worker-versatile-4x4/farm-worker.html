Farm Worker Versatile

The best help a farmer can get

Suzuki welcomes the return of one of New Zealand’s favourite off-road vehicles modified specifically to meet the needs of the modern farmer. The extremely capable 1.3 litre Suzuki Farm Worker with selectable high/low ratio 4WD is set to continue the legend.

With a full canvas cover and folding rear bench seats running lengthwise, this Farm Worker is totally versatile. Whether you need a people mover on the farm or a cover for your tools when you head out the back on rainy winter days, Farm Worker Versatile will see you right.

It even has a forward folding windscreen and the cover can be fully removed for use as an open safari type vehicle. Shown with optional Maxxis tyres fitted – please ask your Suzuki dealer for costs.

The Farm Worker range has been developed to operate as an off-road vehicle only and consequently is not able to be registered for road use.

Warranty: Two year mechanical, one year corrosion. Some exclusions apply. Please ask your Suzuki dealer for details.

$14,990
