SIGNATURE FEATURES

DYNAMIC HANDLING ON ALL TERRAINS

Next-generation Four Corner Air Suspension, and Terrain Response® 2 with five terrain modes, deliver class-leading comfort and capabilities on and off-road.

INDULGE IN THE SOUND OF MERIDIAN™

Meridian™ surround audio system (825w), with 19 Speakers and 16 channels placed around the vehicle, create a concert-like experience for all occupants. (Meridian is a registered trademark of Meridian Audio Ltd.)

DETAILED TOUCHES THAT DEFINE YOUR STYLE

Gloss Black exterior trim detailing, body-coloured sills and bumper corners, and Dark Engine Turned Aluminium Trim Finisher enhance the dynamic design.

A TRULY PERSONALISED INTERIOR

Oxford perforated leather seats, with four-way adjustment and power height adjust for the driver, are available in multiple colourways with an Autobiography pattern.
