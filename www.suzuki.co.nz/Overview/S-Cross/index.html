Size does matter

With a class-leading 430L of boot space, the S-Cross lets you pack more into your day. There's plenty of room for five people to travel in comfort and a quick rearrangement lets you accommodate anything from adventure equipment to baby gear.

With the rear seats down there's a cavernous 875L of space!

Exceptional economy

The S-Cross gives you the perfect combination of performance to meet your everyday needs with exceptional economy thanks to an advanced 1.6 litre VVT engine. It’s more economical, with lower CO2 emissions than many similar sized models, delivering high energy with maximum efficiency.

CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission) provides smooth power delivery and improved fuel efficiency – around town and on the motorway. For total control, select the optional 7- stage ‘steptronic’ mode via the gear lever, or steering wheel mounted paddle shifters (LTD).

  • S-Cross GLX or LTD 2WD 5.8L/100km*
  • S-Cross GLX or LTD AWD 6.2L/100km*

View the Fuel Economy Calculator

*ADR 81/02 results for combined cycle. Fuel consumption will vary due to factors such as vehicle condition, driving style and traffic conditions.

5 star reassurance

Whether you're on or off-road you'll always feel protected in the 5 star safety rated S-Cross. Its impressive list of state-of-the-art safety features includes 7 airbags, Electronic Stability Programme (ESP®), Suzuki's Total Effective Control Technology (TECT), as well as ABS brakes with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD) and Brake Assist System (BAS)

Safety in reverse

Equipped with four ultrasonic parking sensors in the rear bumper and a reverse camera feeding live footage to the dash display, the S-Cross LTD's added safety features guide you easily as you reverse.

One touch technology

Inside the Suzuki S-Cross, modern comforts are just a click away. Keyless entry and push button start technology (LTD) mean you’ll never fumble for your keys again.

Choose your ideal temperature independently of your passengers with digital dual-zone climate control air-conditioning (LTD).

The 6.1 inch touch-screen infotainment display offers voice recognition, audio controls, USB input and Bluetooth® handsfree calling. It also includes a satellite navigation system to ensure you always know where you’re going.

Impressive styling

From the front, the S-Cross is bold and dynamic. From the side, sleek and smooth. With its innovative European body design, the S-Cross is one stylish SUV whichever way you look at it.

Its sharp looks combined with aerodynamic contours contribute to superior fuel economy. The roof rails integrate seamlessly with the roofline and the body coloured door mirrors are shaped for low drag and low wind noise.

Light it up

The automatically adjustable HID (High Intensity Discharge) headlamps with LED position lamps (LTD) are elegantly placed. Finishing touches such as fog lamps accentuated with chrome highlights ensure that no matter where you go, the S-Cross will impress.

ALLGRIP 4WD system

Go all-weather, go all-terrain and get all-round performance with the S-Cross’ fully integrated AWD system – ALLGRIP. At the flick of a switch choose from four driving modes:

  • AUTO for efficient city driving,
  • SNOW for traction on slippery, snowy roads,
  • LOCK for when negotiating muddy or sandy surfaces, and
  • SPORT for the pure fun of driving.
