Designed and tested to Suzuki's specifications, Suzuki Genuine accessories fit seamlessly while maintaining the integrity of your vehicle. Have fun making your Suzuki uniquely yours.

Chrome Protection Package

Part No. 990E0-61M06-PAC

Protect your S-Cross and look good doing it with the chrome protection package, including side body mouldings, rear hatch moulding and front and rear bumper corner protection.

Items can be sold separately. Side Body Moulding Chrome 990E0-61M08-CSM, Rear Hatch Moulding Chrome 990E0-61M09-000, Bumper Corner Protection Front 990E0-61M06-CFR / Rear 990E0-61M06-CRR

Minimum fitting time: 60 minutes

Available on S-Cross GLX 2WD AUTO, S-Cross LTD 2WD AUTO, S-Cross GLX AWD AUTO and S-Cross LTD AWD AUTO.

Rugged Pack

Part No. 990E0-54P00-RUG

Tough, practical and capable. The Vitara can go anywhere and look good doing it. Building on the Vitara's staunch attitude and off-road ability, the rugged accessory pack features front and rear two-tone skid plates, black daytime running light bezels, black body moldings for the sides and a protection guard for the loading edge area.

Items can be sold separately. Large Side Body Moldings Black 990E0-54P07-000, Loading Edge Protection Black 990E0-54P51, Rear Skid Plate 2 Tone 990E0-54P47-000, Front Skid Plate Large 2 Tone 990E0-54P44-001, Daytime Running Light Bezels Black 990E0-54P10-000

Minimum fitting time: 120 minutes

Available on Vitara JLX 2WD MANUAL, Vitara JLX 2WD AUTO, Vitara JLX AWD AUTO, Vitara TURBO 2WD AUTO, Vitara TURBO 2WD TWO TONE, Vitara TURBO AWD AUTO and Vitara TURBO AWD TWO TONE.

Urban Pack

Part No. 990E0-54P00-URB

Sophisticated and stylish, the Vitara is right at home in the city. Now stand out in the crowd with the Vitara urban accessory pack, including chrome plated daytime running light bezels, chrome side body moldings and a colour matched roof spoiler.

Items can be sold separately. Daytime Running Light Bezel Chrome 990E0-54P10-CFB, Side Body Molding Chrome/Black 990E0-54P08-CSM, Roof Edge Spoiler 990E0-54P05-000

Minimum fitting time: 80 minutes

Available on Vitara JLX 2WD MANUAL, Vitara JLX 2WD AUTO, Vitara JLX AWD AUTO, Vitara TURBO 2WD AUTO, Vitara TURBO 2WD TWO TONE, Vitara TURBO AWD AUTO and Vitara TURBO AWD TWO TONE.
