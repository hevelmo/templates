WHAT IS HYBRID?

Range Rover Hybrid is the first diesel SUV of its kind in the world. It has been designed and engineered to deliver class-leading capability and versatility. By fully integrating the hybrid technologies into the chassis, nothing has been lost in ground clearance, approach and departure angles or the 900mm wading depth. The vehicle truly is 100% Hybrid, 100% Land Rover.

WATCH THE FILM

IS HYBRID RIGHT FOR YOU?

Range Rover Hybrid has been tested on the most demanding terrains, rigs and under the same extremes as every Land Rover. Hybrid technologies deliver impressive efficiencies. The 3.0L SDV6 Diesel engine with a 35kW electric motor generates the same performance as the SDV8 Diesel but with 14% lower (27g/km less) CO² emissions when compared to the TDV6 engine at 169g/km.

WATCH THE FILM

THE HYBRID SYSTEM IN MORE DETAIL

Innovative engineering ensures the transmission of energy between battery and engine is seamless. This energy is created by the Regenerative Braking process, which captures and stores kinetic energy in the battery, normally lost through braking. This is then used to power the electric motor and ensures that torque is instantly available on demand.

WATCH THE FILM

CHANGING HYBRID SETTINGS

There are four modes: EV On Mode, EV Off Mode, Sport Mode and Auto Stop/Start. These tailor the vehicle’s performance to your requirements, from optimising battery power to be whisper quiet to enabling the most efficient combination of diesel and electric power.

WATCH THE FILM

ATTENTION TO DETAIL

Attention to detail is at the very heart of Land Rover. That’s why even the alloy wheels on Range Rover Hybrid have been designed for increased efficiency. The design features spokes that are aerodynamically shaped so, as they move through the air, they require less power to keep them moving. To finish, the vehicle is specified with unique Hybrid badges located in the side vent and rear tailgate.
