XF LUXURY

Combine striking contemporary style with superb craftsmanship.

Más colores disponibles para este modelo.

 

A range of functions adds greater convenience, from the 7 inch colour Touch-screen display to the Jaguar Smart Key System™ with Keyless start. Enjoy a contemporary exterior look with 17 inch Ursa alloy rines.

Listen to your favourite music on the crystal clear Jaguar 250W Sound System, front DVD and virtual multi-changer. Reach every destination more easily with XF's navigation system with HDD mapping.

ENGINE:

2.0 LITRE i4 240 TURBOCHARGED PETROL

Experience the economy and performance of a 4 cylinder engine with the refinement and excitement usually found with 6 cylinders. This state-of-the-art turbocharged engine provides outstanding driveability with strong low rpm performance and a broad power band. It achieves 0-100km/h in 7.9 seconds with 240PS of power and 340Nm torque.

Fuel Economy (Combined): Up to 8.1 l/100km

CO2 Emissions: 189 g/km

Transmission: 8-Speed Transmission with Intelligent Stop/Start

Engine Performance

3.0 LITRE V6 340 SUPERCHARGED PETROL

Discover the power of V8 engine architecture with V6 efficiency and minimal loss of performance prowess. Enjoy enhanced power and performance with a range of improved efficiency fuel and induction technologies including direct injection and twin variable camshaft timing. With power reaching 340PS and 450Nm torque, it achieves 0-100km/h in just 5.3 seconds.

Fuel Economy (Combined): Up to 9.6 l/100km

CO2 Emissions: 221g/km

Transmission: 8 Speed Transmission with Intelligent Stop/Start

Engine Performance

3.0 LITRE V6 340 SUPERCHARGED PETROL AWD

The 3.0 litre V6 340PS Supercharged AWD petrol engine with its instinctive Jaguar All Wheel Drive (AWD) technology gives the driver confidence in all conditions. It can accelerate from 0-100km/h in 5.8 seconds with CO2 emissions of 229g/km.

Fuel Economy (Combined): Up to 9.6 l/100km

CO2 Emissions: 229g/km

Transmission: 8 Speed Transmission with Intelligent Stop/Start

Engine Performance

CARACTERISTICAS DE SERIE

Jaguar Smart Key System™ with Keyless Start

Feel your XF come to life with the Keyless Start. One press of the start button and the engine purrs into life.

Jaguar 250W Sound System

Listen to your favourite tracks from the radio, external device or CDs. No distortion or compression, just moving music from 8 high-efficiency speakers with a high-mounted woofer and tweeter in each door.

Bluetooth® audio streaming

Advanced Bluetooth® technology links compatible mobile phones to the in-built hands-free telephone system and lets you stream your favourite music through the high quality audio system.

Bond grain seats with six-way electric adjustment

The convenience and comfort of 6-way electric seat adjustment with the added luxury of Bond grain leather.

Phosphor blue halo illumination and interior mood lighting

Phosphor blue halo illumination picks out the switches, JaguarDrive Selector™, start button, Electric Parking Brake (EPB) and instrument panel, as well as the door switches, centre and upper consoles.

Exterior mirrors, heated with electric adjustment

Ensure a superior rear-view in cold weather with heated electric door mirrors.

Rear parking aid

Ultrasonic sensors in the car’s rear bumper detect the proximity of objects as you reverse to provide an audible warning along with simultaneous graphics on the Touch-screen.

CARACTERISTICAS OPCIONALES

Jaguar Smart Key System™ with Keyless entry

Keyless entry lets you into your XF without pressing a button. Simply unlock and disarm the vehicle by operating the door handle. You can also lock your vehicle with a simple press of the keyless button.

Meridian™ 380W Sound System

Enjoy the finest audio on the move with this high quality sound system from market leaders Meridian™, featuring 380W power with 11 speakers and single slot CD/DVD.

Meridian™ 825W Surround Sound System

Bring your music to life with 17 loudspeakers and Surround Sound Processing that creates superb stereo throughout the cabin. While Meridian™ Cabin Correction (MCC) delivers a smoother, more natural sound, adding clarity and definition to every instrument. Dynamic Volume Control enhances your music quality still further, monitoring and compensating for changes in the cabin’s ambient sound levels. And exclusive Trifield technology delivers a full, wide stereo experience for both driver and passenger.

Adaptive Front Lighting with Cornering Lamps

Optional Adaptive Front Lighting features dynamic headlights that react to speed and steering.

Electric glass sunroof with tilt/slide one touch operation

Open your sunroof – or simply tilt for ventilation – at the push of a button.

Electric rear window sunblind

Conveniently reduce sun glare with this electrically operated sunblind.

Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC)

For safer and smoother journeys, Adaptive Cruise Control’s radar senses if a vehicle ahead slows and adjusts your speed to maintain a safe distance. Intelligent Emergency Braking and Active Seat Belts detect how firmly each needs to be applied during emergency braking.

Adaptive Dynamics

Sharpen and hone XF’s impressive handling even further. Adaptive Dynamics continually monitors the car’s speed, steering and body movements, analysing the suspension response and varying the damping to suit the conditions. The system calculates the suspension response 500 times every second to keep your XF flat, stable and secure.

*Not available on all models

Chrome side power vents

Give your XF even more dynamic appeal with these stylish chrome side power vents.

Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)

Maintaining optimum tyre pressure is vital to your XF’s efficiency and handling. Optional Tyre Pressure Monitoring provides an automatic alert if pressure drops significantly.

Ski Hatch

Use this handy hatch to make room for your skis or other long items without lowering the seats.

Blind Spot Monitor

Radar sensors take the uncertainty out of changing lanes with a warning signal any time another car enters the driver’s blind spot. An icon in the appropriate external mirror illuminates as soon a vehicle is detected when you choose the optional Blind Spot Monitor.

Intelligent High Beam

Drive more safely at night. Your XF automatically switches between high and low beam as cars approach. Once they have passed, it automatically switches back to high beam.

Front and rear parking aid with camera

Get a better perspective when you’re parking thanks to the ultrasonic sensors in the front bumper. You’ll hear a warning if you get within 1.8m of an object. For added safety and ease of parking, the optional rear-facing video camera shows you what’s behind the car on your 7 inch Touch-screen.
