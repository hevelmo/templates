INCONTROL APPS

Please note that as we systematically introduce InControl, specific features, options and its availability remain market dependent. Please consult your country-specific Land Rover website or local Retailer for availability in your country.

HOW TO OPERATE INCONTROL APPS

Once connected to your smartphone, InControl Apps can be controlled from your vehicle’s Touch-screen. To find out how to install InControl Apps and search for compatible apps, please consult your country-specific Land Rover website or local Retailer. Once your chosen apps are downloaded, connect your smartphone via the USB port within the vehicle to use them through the vehicle’s Touch-screen.

COMPATIBLE APPS AND DEVICES

InControl Apps is available on most Android and Apple iOS smartphones. InControl Apps works seamlessly with your vehicle’s Touch-screen. Plan, discover, and stay connected to your vehicle-optimised Apps.

FIND OUT MORE ABOUT THE DEVICES YOU CAN USE WITH INCONTROL APPS.

INCONTROL VEHICLE-OPTIMISED APPS

InControl Apps enables you to use a variety of vehicle-optimised smartphone apps on your Touch-screen.

  • CITYSEEKER

    Cityseeker acts as your personal concierge for travel information and city guides when you’re on the go. Discover over 500 cities worldwide, the hotels, restaurants, attractions and cultural hotspots. Authoritative travel content for you, on the road and always at your fingertips.

  • EVENTSEEKER

    Eventseeker is your personalised in-car event service. It minimises searching and maximises discovery so you’ll never miss what’s right around the corner. Whether you are new to a city or a local, we tap into your social likes and music libraries to recommend events in any city you’re driving through.

  • HOTELSEEKER

    Hotelseeker lets business and leisure travellers easily discover and book accommodation around the world. A comprehensive selection of accommodation worldwide means you’ll find the perfect place to rest your head. Hotelseeker guarantees complete listings catering to your preference of brand, rating and cost.

  • MOBILEDAY – COMING SOON

    MobileDay is a smartphone application that auto-syncs to your mobile calendar. It connects calls using One-Touch with the correct dialling sequence to any meeting on any conference service. This eliminates the need to look-up, memorise and dial in conference numbers while driving.

  • NEWS ON BOARD

    News on Board uses text-to-speech technology to offer high quality news content collated from leading global news agencies into six separate categories, keeping you abreast of developments in real time while you’re on the road.

  • PARKOPEDIA

    Parkopedia is the world’s largest parking information provider used by millions of drivers every month online and through its smartphone app. Parkopedia provides detailed information on 28 million parking spaces in over 90,000 facilities across 6,000 cities in 40 countries.

  • SYGIC

    Sygic is an offline GPS navigation system, helping 33 million people every day travel confidently and safely in 115 countries.

  • MILEAGE TRACKER PRO

    Keep track of your commutes with Mileage Tracker Pro. With this handy app, you can see all the statistics on your most frequent trips, such as how long they took, amount of miles, and the amount of fuel used.

  • STITCHER

    Stitcher allows you to listen to your favourite news, comedy, sports and talk radio shows on demand. Discover the best of NPR, CNN, Fox, BBC, WSJ, Adam Carolla, Joe Rogan, Rachel Maddow, Rush Limbaugh, Fresh Air, Freakonomics, Radiolab and over 20,000 shows, podcasts and live stations.

  • AUDIOBOOKS

    This free app has a library of over 45,000 free audiobooks including bestsellers, new releases and classics in every genre. Download or stream an audiobook and start listening to a great story.

  • NEWSBEAT

    Listen to a playlist of succinct 1-minute news stories in a NPR-like streaming radio experience. Listen to only the stories you like, and skip the rest. Personalise the playlist with your favourite topics and news sources. Catch up on news during your commute.

  • RIVET RADIO

    Rivet Radio is the perfect companion for your morning commute, at the gym or on-the-go. Stay informed on everything going on in your world - that means local, national and global news delivered straight to your smartphone.

  • GLYMPSE - COMING SOON

    Glympse is a fast, free and simple way to share your location using GPS tracking in real time with friends and family!

  • RDIO - COMING SOON

    Rdio provides over 35 million songs to discover, play, and share. With Rdio, you can enjoy endless stations based on artists, genres, moods and themes, along with You FM, your own personalized station, play albums, songs, and playlists or download songs for offline listening.
