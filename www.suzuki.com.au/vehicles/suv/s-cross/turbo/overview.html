BOOSTERJET TURBO ENGINE

Turbo Performance

The new S-Cross Turbo and Turbo Prestige both come with a powerful 1.4 litre Boosterjet Turbo engine – so superior performance and driving pleasure go hand-in-hand.

Helping generate greater torque from lower speeds, the new Turbo engine’s enhanced efficiency helps achieve an economical fuel consumption rate of just 5.9L/100km*. In fact, from city peak hour to country back roads, the Boosterjet Turbo engine optimises your power and efficiency wherever you’re travelling, while significant structural weight savings result in an impressive power-to-weight ratio that will also help you save at the pump.

Coupled with a 6-speed automatic transmission, the new S-Cross Turbo raises the bar for smooth driving performance across the board.

*ADR 81/02 results for automatic transmission. NEDC Fuel Consumption figures may not mimic real-life driving conditions and should be considered for comparison against other vehicles only.

Hi-Tech Meets High Style

Sleek, flowing surfaces and a wide, uncluttered dash interface make driving an immersive and enjoyable experience.

A new, highly advanced yet simple-to-follow multimedia satellite navigation system – with Apple CarPlay® and a reversing camera – puts knowledge, pleasure and control at your fingertips. Simply tap the user-friendly 7-inch touchscreen or use voice recognition to plan your route, call your friends or enjoy your favourite music.

Shift paddles let you take control of the driving experience and change gears without letting go of the steering wheel, whilst the cruise control helps you maintain a driver-set speed.

Steering wheel-mounted audio and Bluetooth controls are also practical and convenient, allowing the driver to concentrate on the road.

Travel In Style

Space to Spare

The new S-Cross Turbo’s innovative, intelligent design gives you the flexibility to carry more things to more places, but still leaves you with plenty of room to move. So whether you’ve got a big load of ‘stuff’ or you’re packing for a big holiday, S-Cross Turbo gives you freedom as well as functionality.

Settle in and get comfortable. The roomy seats expand the feeling of space and comfort, whilst the Turbo Prestige adds a touch of luxury with leather accented seats. Continue to cosy up with an array of creature comforts with a push button start and dual-zone climate control.

The S-Cross also provides 430 litres of luggage space – one of the largest capacities in the category, even with five people on board – the new S-Cross Turbo SUV gives you space to spare, and then some.

The versatile cargo bay is simple to rearrange, creating enough room for everything or anything. You can even start packing for a weekend getaway with all the smart space you need for all-out enjoyment.

Safe and Sure

In the S-Cross Turbo, exceptional performance is crossed with advanced safety features.

Enjoy the protection that comes with 7 airbags (dual front, side and curtain airbags and a knee airbag for the driver), Electronic Stability Control (ESC) and Suzuki’s Total Effective Control Technology (TECT), while ABS brakes with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD) and Brake Assist System (BAS) deliver reassuring stopping power.

Get into the new S-Cross and you and your family will be surrounded by safety at every turn.
