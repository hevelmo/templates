Performance reignited

Vitara is Suzuki’s ultimate expression of the compact SUV. Whether choosing the fuel efficient 1.6 litre model or a flagship Turbo version, the new Vitara is sure to impress.

The Vitara Turbo with its muscular 1.4 litre BoosterJet engine, delivers 20% more power and a massive 40% more instant torque. Reaching impressive performance levels while still delivering amazing fuel economy of only 5.9 litres per 100km (2WD combined cycle), the new Vitara Turbo is performance…reignited.

Technology reinvented

Listen to your music, call your friends or plan a journey; Vitara’s multimedia satellite navigation system with Apple CarPlay®* and Bluetooth® makes it easy by connecting your smartphone seamlessly to your Vitara. Intuitive and uncomplicated, the interface connects via voice recognition or with a 7-inch multi-touch display, making driving safer and simply more enjoyable.

*Apple and CarPlay are trademarks of Apple Inc. Apple CarPlay is compatible with iPhone 5 and above.

Style redesigned

Stylishly rugged, this modern SUV is right at home in the city, on the open highway, or off the beaten track. It’s muscular body, slick roof rails and bold front grille redefines comfort, practicality and style. The Vitara goes anywhere and looks good doing it.

Interior redefined

The spacious interior of the Vitara redefines compact SUV comfort and driving intelligence, with a high level of finish throughout. The dynamic dash design integrates the 7-inch multimedia screen with reversing camera and intelligent multimedia satellite navigation system.

Roomy and flexible, the Vitara is designed to fit in with your lifestyle with a generous 375 litres of luggage space, 60:40 folding seats, a movable luggage board and a lower tailgate for extra easy loading.

Individuality reimagined

Whether you are looking for a few final touches for the interior or more significant enhancements for the exterior, there is no shortage of choice, or opportunities, to make the Vitara truly your own.

Take a look at the wide range of accessories available for your new Vitara.

Adventure rediscovered

Don’t let the Vitara AWD’s good looks fool you. Under the stylish exterior is a more than capable, off-road performer. With fully integrated ALLGRIP technology, adventure has no limits as you navigate roads less travelled with sure-footed confidence, control and handling. Four selected modes:

  • AUTO for efficient city driving,
  • SNOW for traction on slippery, snowy roads,
  • LOCK for when negotiating muddy or sandy surfaces, and
  • SPORT for the pure fun of driving.

Efficiency​​ reaffirmed

A high-riding, roomy SUV with the fuel efficiency normally associated with a compact car at 5.8L/100kms*, the economical Vitara fits in a lot more adventure between fills. Plus, with a suspension tuned to ensure the best possible combination of stability, comfort and quietness, you are ready to head down any road.

Available in either smooth-shifting 5-speed manual or the newly developed 6-stage automatic.

  • Vitara JLX 2WD Manual 5.8L/100km*
  • Vitara Turbo 2WD Auto 5.9L/100km*
  • Vitara JLX or LTD 2WD Auto 6.0L/100km*
  • Vitara Turbo AWD Auto 6.1L/100km*
  • Vitara JLX or LTD AWD Auto 6.3L/100km*

View the Fuel Economy Calculator

*ADR 81/02 results for combined cycle. Fuel consumption will vary due to factors such as vehicle condition, driving style and traffic conditions.

Safety reassured

Inside the Vitara’s quiet, well designed cabin, you get a reassuring sense of protection thanks to a combination of TECT body design, 7 airbags and a suite of electronic systems including ABS with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD), and Electronic Stability Programme (ESP®).

Hill descent control
If you’re on a steep slope or in a difficult off-road situation let the Vitara’s hill descent control regulate the speed travelling downhill (AWD).

Hill hold control
If you’re on an incline, the brake is automatically applied until you move forward to prevent you rolling backwards. This makes hill starts easy in the Vitara, even in the most challenging conditions (Auto).

Cruise control and speed limiter
Cruise control maintains your speed in the Vitara to a set level, while the speed limiter function enables you to set a maximum speed for safe driving.

Fog lamps and LED daylight running lamps
Essential for good road visibility in poor weather the Vitara’s fog lamps are accentuated by the automatic daytime LED driving lamps.

Reverse camera
The reverse camera matched to the 7-inch touchscreen display allows you to reverse safely, while the added front and rear proximity sensors on the LTD model guides you with audible warnings.
