SIGNATURE FEATURES

DISTINCTIVE ALLOY WHEELS

Land Rover Discovery SE is fitted with 19-inch ten-spoke alloy wheels.

HEIGHT ADJUSTABLE AIR SUSPENSION

Air suspension with ride levelling flexes to suit your needs. Raise the ride height to 125mm for tackling difficult paths or lower it 50mm for easier loading.

FLEXIBLE TO FIT

Whatever your plans, Discovery’s generous interior is configurable to suit almost any use, with the second and third row of seats folding independently of each other.

PRACTICAL FEATURES, BEAUTIFULLY DESIGNED

There is a choice of Almond or Ebony cloth seats to suit your tastes.
