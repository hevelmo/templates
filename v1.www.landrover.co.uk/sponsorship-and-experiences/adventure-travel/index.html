PARTNERED WITH EXPERTS

Two iconic British brands, each famous for luxury and exploration, have come together to form Land Rover Adventure Travel by Abercrombie & Kent.

LUXURY HOLIDAYS FULL OF ADVENTURE

Fuel your spirit of adventure with a bespoke self-drive holiday. See some of the world’s most iconic destinations from behind the wheel of a Land Rover.

IN THE HANDS OF TRAVEL EXPERTS

Each adventure has been planned with the meticulous detail, local insight and seamless ground arrangements for which Abercrombie & Kent is renowned.

OFF THE BEATEN TRACK

You may be off the beaten track but you’re never on your own. The reassuring presence of Land Rover’s team of qualified instructors enables you to enjoy the landscapes in the comfort and luxury of the latest Land Rover vehicle.

GREAT BRITAIN

This unique journey through England’s historic landscapes and heritage started at the Savoy Hotel in London. From there, guests went on to explore glorious British countryside taking in castles and manors en route. This was followed by two days at Eastnor Castle in the tranquil Malvern Hills which include off-road driving on the legendary Eastnor tracks.
