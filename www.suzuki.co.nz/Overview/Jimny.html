Go anywhere 4X4

For forty years it’s been the 4WD that’s gone places the others can’t. Built on a sturdy, 3 section, ladder frame chassis, you can push it further and harder than any ‘soft-roader’ you see around town. The live front and rear axles combine with 3-link coil suspension and separate shocks for unstoppable ground clearance and rugged go-anywhere ability.

Effortless performance

Featuring a compact yet muscular all-aluminium engine, the Jimny delivers effortless power at 62.4/6,000 kW/rpm. The twincam 1,328cm3 powerplant spins freely to high revs, providing plenty of torque and instant response over almost any terrain.

Simple comfort

The rugged Jimny is full of practical comforts. Driver controls are within easy reach, meters are clear and simple, and its high seating position gives you a commanding view ahead.

A sliding front passenger seat, wide doors and low sills enable easy access and there’s plenty of room for gear. The Jimny’s rear door opens vertically to 90 degrees plus each side of the rear seat folds independently to create space for odd shapes such as fishing and camping equipment. Larger items can be secured on the roof rails (Sierra).

The Sierra model has a range of comfort items including electric windows and mirrors, remote central locking, and Bluetooth compatible audio system to make the most of your time on adventure.

Safety first

Jimny’s stable handling, powerful braking, and quick acceleration give you total control both on and off-road. In sticky situations a host of electronic systems help keep you safe. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) ensures safer cornering by reducing understeer or oversteer. Brake Assist (BA) maximises braking pressure in an emergency, while Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) improves braking efficiency for reduced stopping distances and increased control.

The Jimny’s body and frame are designed to absorb the force of a frontal collision and spread it away from occupants. Side door beams maintain cabin integrity and there are dual front airbags for added safety.

4WD capability, 2WD economy

No matter how much you enjoy going off road, your Jimny will spend at least some of its time on sealed roads. For this reason, the Jimny has a selectable 4×4 system. When the going isn’t as tough, you can select 2WD for increased fuel economy. 1.3L 4-cylinder petrol engine.

Go manual or auto

Jimny offers a choice of transmissions: five-speed manual and four-speed automatic. Engine power reaches the transmission smoothly thanks to a specially designed chain that reduces friction, noise, and power losses. A lockup feature for the automatic transmission boosts fuel economy at highway speeds.
