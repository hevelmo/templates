EXPLORAR MÁS

2015, DEFENDER

RUGBY WORLD CUP TROPHY TOUR

BY Land Rover 2015

River Thames, United Kingdom TO Whitton Rd, Twickenham TW2 7BA, United Kingdom 5413 km

As Worldwide Partner and Official Vehicle Partner of Rugby World Cup 2015, Land Rover's Special Vehicle Operations team created a unique Defender to transport the Webb Ellis Cup on a 100-day Domestic Trophy Tour across the UK and Ireland.

The 110 Station Wagon was specially modified to feature an illuminated Trophy cabinet, with state-of-the-art technology to control temperature and locking mechanism to keep the cabinet safely in place for the duration of the tour.

The tour began in a breathtaking fashion in London, UK as the Defender was unveiled when it drove out of an 8.2 metre high rugby ball which floated 25 miles up the River Thames from Tilbury Dock. The Rugby World Cup Trophy Tour, around the UK and Ireland, reflected the Defender's legendary 'go anywhere, do anything' character as it traversed countrywide to bring the Web Ellis Cup to over 300 events in 100 days and engage with fans and players at all levels.
