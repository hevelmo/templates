As you would expect from a Discovery, the versatile Discovery Sport will feature 5+2 seating, while still retaining a more compact footprint than traditional 5-seat SUVs.

Gerry McGovern Land Rover Design Director and Chief Creative Officer

THE EXTRAORDINARY EVERY DAY

New Discovery Sport's sleek, aerodynamic silhouette, with legendary Land Rover capability and a flexible interior, add up to a great looking vehicle that does so much more.

EXTREME TESTED FOR DRIVER CONFIDENCE

New Discovery Sport features all-new safety technologies, such as Pedestrian Airbag and Autonomous Emergency Braking System allowing Discovery Sport to achieve maximum five star safety rating from Euro NCAP.

FLEXIBLE SEATING, LEGROOM AND LOADSPACE

With a sliding second row that can offer similar legroom to a Range Rover, and a third row you can adapt the space to meet loading or seating needs.

HI-TECH DISPLAYS FOR EASE OF USE

Cutting edge controls distinguish an uncluttered dashboard that features an eight-inch Touch-screen with the latest technology for intuitive interaction.

VERSATILITY

VERSATILITY

GENEROUS ADAPTABLE LOADSPACE

New Discovery Sport has been designed and engineered to meet any challenge. With a loadspace of up to 1698 litres and the option of flexible seating which, when stored flat, gives a load floor that is as long as a Range Rover, it accommodates passengers and their kit with ease.

VERSATILITY

DECEPTIVELY SPACIOUS

Supremely comfortable second row stadium seating offers high visibility and a sector-leading 160mm of travel. The 5+2 third row means you can fit in two little ones sitting comfortably in the back. It features a simple, single-handed 50:50 deployment and can be fitted without compromising exterior design.

INNOVATION

Popup content

  • A CONFIDENT EMPOWERING DRIVE

    New Discovery Sport bristles with all the advances you’ll need to make driving a real pleasure. Features such as, Dynamic Stability Control, Roll Stability Control and Corner Brake Control actively monitor the vehicle’s behaviour to deliver the most assured, confident drive imaginable so you always feel in control.

    WATCH VIDEO

  • PEDESTRIAN AIRBAG

    New Discovery Sport features a state-of-the-art pedestrian airbag system. Designed and tested to the most rigorous standards including advanced virtual and real-world situations, it protects pedestrians from impact with the base of the windscreen by using an airbag mounted within a cover found at the trailing edge of the bonnet panel.

    WATCH VIDEO

  • AUTONOMOUS EMERGENCY BRAKING

    New Discovery Sport offers an innovative emergency braking system, using stereo cameras analysed by the on-board computer to calculate collision risk. You are alerted by warning signals, and emergency brakes will be applied automatically. Under 31mph it helps avoid collisions, up to 50mph it may mitigate severity.

    WATCH VIDEO

  • FUEL EFFICIENCY WITH ECO MODE

    An optional driver-selected feature which prioritises the most efficient vehicle settings including auto transmission+ and comfort features like heating and air conditioning in order to minimise fuel consumption and CO2 emissions. The Touch-screen displays data relating to your driving efficiency.

    +Where fitted.

  • PACKAGING

    New Discovery Sport features significant developments in packaging, including 5+2 stadium style seating and new multi-link rear suspension. This has been designed to minimise intrusion into the cabin, delivering even greater space and versatility.

    WATCH VIDEO

  • PARKING MADE SIMPLE

    Perfect for the city, Park Assist is a suite of features including Parallel Park, Perpendicular Park and Parking Exit to help you both enter and leave your chosen space safely. Using ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles close by, you just control the accelerator, gears and brake while the vehicle does the steering for you.
