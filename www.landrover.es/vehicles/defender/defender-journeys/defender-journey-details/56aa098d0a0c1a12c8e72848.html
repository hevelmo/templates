1983, 90 - 130

Camel Trophy - Brazil

BY Camel Trophy by Land Rover 1989

Alta Floresta, Alta Floresta - MT, Brazil TO Manaus - State of Amazonas, Brazil 2661 km

In 1989, Camel Trophy’s penultimate expedition returned to Brazil once again for what was considered the toughest challenge to date – a jeopardous journey through the Amazon rainforest in the middle of the rainy season.

The route was so hazardous that the locals believed the expedition was completely impassable due to the flooded riverbank and ever-challenging terrain. The Land Rover 110 proved them wrong.

After an unrelenting 1,410km of rainforest, the battered but tenacious 110s arrived in Manaus – with British brothers Bob & Joe Ives taking the 1989 trophy.
