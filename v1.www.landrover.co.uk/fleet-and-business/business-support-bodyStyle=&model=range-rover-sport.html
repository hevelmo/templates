Your first port of call – the Land Rover Fleet & Business Centre

This dedicated centre provides highly experienced personnel to service all our fleet and business customers. Here, the advisors can support you on anything from choosing the best Land Rover for your drivers, through company car tax considerations and emissions targets to different finance options.

Once sure they fully understand your requirements, they’ll put you in touch with the best contact for your business in one of our specialist service areas – Fleet & Business Sales Managers (for medium and large fleets) or Fleet & Business Dealers (for smaller fleets of up to 24 vehicles).

We deal with every detail of an order meticulously and are used to working with the demands of all kinds of business types, from utilities to the emergency services. From the moment you join us, you’ll have the same named person to help, advise and guide you on all your fleet needs.

PEACE OF MIND

Excellent value, any way you look at it


No matter which Land Rover you choose, it’ll be exceptionally capable and reliable. But when you’re running a business, it’s good to know you’re covered. That’s why every single Land Rover comes with all this included:

  • Three years’ Roadside Assistance in the UK and all across Europe, with a 24-hour helpline – 0800 521 786
  • Three year warranty with unlimited mileage – so you can roam across Europe or into the wilds
  • Six year anti-perforation warranty, to protect against rust – a must when you’re blazing new trails in the snow or wet


We’ll take care of all maintenance for you


To keep everything running smoothly, tap into the expertise of our Approved Service Centres. Your specialist contact at Land Rover will take care of this for you but you can find your nearest centre here.

They have the training, expert equipment, correct tools for the job and all the genuine Land Rover parts to keep your Land Rover in the very best condition.

And here’s what your three year warranty covers


Almost every square inch is covered. So if your Land Rover does encounter an issue as a result of a faulty part, it’ll be repaired or replaced free at your nearest Approved Service Centre.

Tyres come under the manufacturer’s guarantee, so they’re excluded, but your local Land Rover dealer will happily help you make a claim if you need to. And while your Land Rover is being brought back to its best, you can still get the job done – we’ll make sure you have a courtesy car ready.
