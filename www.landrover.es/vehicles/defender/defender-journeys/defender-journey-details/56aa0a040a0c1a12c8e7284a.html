1953, SERIES I

The Beak Family Trip

BY The Beak Family by Land Rover 1953

Bath, Bath, Bath and North East Somerset, UK TO Zimbabwe 12384 km

Garage owner Don Beak bought a second-hand Series I Land Rover and set off on a 7,000-mile trek to find 'a better life' with his wife and two children. From Bath in Somerset, UK, they drove through Europe, crossed to North Africa then down through the middle of the continent to Zimbabwe (then Southern Rhodesia). Crossing desert, mountains, jungle and bush along the way. With a homemade 35-gallon water tank bolted to the roof, the Beak's Land Rover performed faultlessly the entire way.
