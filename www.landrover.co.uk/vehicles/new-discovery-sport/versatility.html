New Discovery Sport takes versatility to new heights.

New Discovery Sport has been developed to be the most versatile and capable vehicle in its category. Its 5+2 stadium seating, sliding second row and rear seats that fold flat gives you the flexibility to accommodate almost any load with ease.

So whether you want to just take the family on an adventure, or friends and their sporting gear off to the game, you can be sure the vehicle delivers.

New Discovery Sport ready for a Family Adventure

5+2 Seating

Enjoy greater flexibility, the third row with its easy to use lift and release seating is ideal for children and teenagers. When folded, the third row becomes a flat floor space that gives a load floor as long as a Range Rover. However you choose to configure the vehicle, it can accommodate passengers and their kit with ease, helping you get out there and live life to the fullest.


Flexible Seating

To help you make the most of the available space, the second row Slide & Recline seating has a forward and backward movement of 160mm, giving you as much leg room as a Range Rover if needed. This ensures you and your family can enjoy greater comfort or utilise the enhanced area for more luggage, making the vehicle deceptively spacious. As the rear seats can also recline, they give you more options without compromising the exterior design.


Loadspace

Versatility is at the heart of New Discovery Sport. With rear seats that fold completely flat and the Slide & Recline second row New Discovery Sport can handle whatever you through in it. You can seat five people comfortably and have 981 litres of loadspace or with all rear seats flat have 1698 litres.

No matter what or who you want to carry, this class-leading vehicle holds its own.
