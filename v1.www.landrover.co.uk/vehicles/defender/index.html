Defender is the epitome of toughness, of ruggedness, of strength and most importantly of capability.

Phil Popham Group Marketing Director, Jaguar Land Rover

TOUGH ADAPTABILITY

Land Rover Defender combines superb capability, ruggedness and dependability in one. As assured in the city as it is out in the wilderness.

IMMENSE STRENGTH

Defender’s legendary strength and durability are underpinned by a robust design including a tough and adaptable box-section ladder-frame chassis.

AT HOME ON ANY TERRAIN

Permanent four-wheel drive, with the centre differential distributing torque evenly between front and rear axles, delivers optimum traction on all terrains.

THE BESPOKE VEHICLE

Defender is available in no fewer than eight core body styles and three wheelbase lengths, while a range of extras and accessories allow further personalisation.

CAPABILITY

AN ICON FOR OVER 65 YEARS

All-terrain right from the start, Land Rover Defender rapidly became one of the world’s most recognisable four-wheel drive vehicles. Enduring and capable, it is a legend on seven continents for challenging expeditions and humanitarian support.

ALL-TERRAIN CAPABILITY

With maximum capability in all conditions and over the most challenging surfaces, Land Rover Defender is used by emergency services around the world. Permanent four-wheel drive ensures a steady safer ride over muddy fields and rocky tracks. On the road, the drive is composed and acceleration assured.

MAXIMUM MUSCLE FOR TOUGH ENVIRONMENTS

Truly adaptable, the 2.2 litre diesel engine produces an impressive 360Nm of torque. With 90% of peak power constantly on tap, it provides all the torque you need to cope with the challenges that many operations demand.

PROFESSIONAL PULLING POWER

With the muscle and articulation you need for big loads, Land Rover Defender can be relied upon to deliver over any distance. The 360Nm of torque generated by the diesel engine supports an impressive braked trailer towing capability of 3,500kg.

CARRIES WHATEVER YOU WANT TO CARRY

The versatile interior space, roof or chassis can be optimised to accommodate the most complex loads and to transport them safely to your destination. Whatever your carrying requirements, Land Rover Defender can meet the challenge.

CAPABILITY

TACKLES THE STEEPEST INCLINE WITH EASE

Land Rover Defender is designed to tackle challenging climbs and descents. The dual-range transfer box provides an additional six forward and one reverse low-range gears for gradients as steep as 45 degrees, while the centre differential can be locked to minimise wheel spin and maximise traction on slippery surfaces.

SPECIFICATIONS AT A GLANCE

MAXIMUM GRADIENT

45°

Designed to tackle extreme climbs and descents with ease, the centre differential can be locked to help minimise wheel spin and maximise traction.

AXLE GROUND CLEARANCE

250MM

Defender has the clearance, chassis and articulation to overcome the most inhospitable terrains.

TORQUE
