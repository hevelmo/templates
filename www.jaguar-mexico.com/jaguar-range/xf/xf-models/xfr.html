XFR

Enjoy the rewards of 5.0 litre Supercharged performance, the ride and handling of Adaptive Dynamics and an Active Differential combined with the luxury of world-class craftsmanship.

More colours are available for this model.

 

Exclusive ‘R’ body styling adds a unique front bumper with larger air intakes, a front grille with black mesh inserts, heat-extracting bonnet louvres and rear spoiler. Stylish Nevis 20 inch alloy wheels complete the sporting appeal.

Soft grain leather sports seats offer 18/18 way electric adjustment for driver and passenger. Heated and cooled, they also feature a memory function with two settings for the driver’s seat.

5.0 LITRE V8 510 SUPERCHARGED PETROL

Experience the driving power of a sixth generation Eaton supercharger available on the XFR. Achieves 0-100km/h in just 4.9 seconds, with a remarkable 510PS and 625Nm torque.

Fuel Economy (combined): Up to 11.6 l/100km

CO2 Emissions: 270g/km

Transmission: 8 Speed Transmission

Engine Performance

STANDARD FEATURES

Unique rear R valance, rear spoiler and quad exhaust tailpipes

Sports-inspired finishing touch, quad tailpipes complete the look.

Meridian™ 825W Surround Sound System

Bring your music to life on this premium 825W surround sound system from market leaders Meridian™ with 17 speakers and single slot CD/DVD.

Bluetooth® audio streaming

Advanced Bluetooth® technology links compatible mobile phones to the in-built hands-free telephone system and lets you stream your favourite music through the high quality audio system.

Navigation system with HDD mapping

Enjoy faster searches and route calculations with the optional hard disk navigation system. Steer clear of unexpected delays with immediate congestion alerts, alternative route suggestions and even receive nearby filling station locations when fuel is running low.

SD Mapping available for certain markets.

OPTIONAL FEATURES

Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC)

For safer and smoother journeys, Adaptive Cruise Control’s radar senses if a vehicle ahead slows and adjusts your speed to maintain a safe distance. Intelligent Emergency Braking and Active Seat Belts detect how firmly each needs to be applied during emergency braking.

Reverse park camera with guidance

Ease into smaller parking spaces, day or night. A video camera concealed in the car's rear lets you see what's behind you, while sensors in the rear bumper alert you to objects close behind via your Touch-screen.

Multimedia system – with front digital/analogue television tuner

Expand your media options with a range of digital and analogue TV channels for viewing on the Touch-screen.

*Knurled Aluminium finish is unavailable on XFR and XFR-S

Interactive Voice

Use spoken commands to control the navigation systems, audio and climate control.

Ski Hatch

Use this handy hatch to make room for your skis or other long items without lowering the seats.
