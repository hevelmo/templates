Cargo Organiser

Part No. 990E0-54P21-000

Keep your personal items clean, tidy and secure with this lightweight all weather cargo organiser tailor made to perfectly fit under the luggage board of your Vitara. Separated into storage compartments with aluminium dividers, the cargo organiser protects the boot area from wet and dirty items such as umbrellas, beach towels and sports equipment and prevents items from sliding around the boot. It is easily removed for cleaning and once the luggage board is secured on top, items are safely stored away from prying eyes.

Minimum fitting time: 20 minutes

Available on Vitara JLX 2WD MANUAL, Vitara JLX 2WD AUTO, Vitara JLX AWD AUTO, Vitara TURBO 2WD AUTO, Vitara TURBO 2WD TWO TONE, Vitara TURBO AWD AUTO and Vitara TURBO AWD TWO TONE.
