SIGNATURE FEATURES

TOP QUALITY INTERIOR

Seats are upholstered in high-quality grained leather in a choice of three colourways for a natural look and durability.

LIGHT AND AIRY SPACE

The fixed panoramic roof brings the outside in and floods the interior with natural light.

DUAL VIEW TOUCH-SCREEN

This optional technology enables both the driver and front passenger to view independent display content via the Touch-screen. For example, while the driver is guided by the navigation system, the front seat passenger can watch a DVD.

Available with the Entertainment Pack

ADAPTIVE XENON HEADLAMPS

These optional headlamps swivel to follow the road, while static bending lamps light the kerbside area in tight bends to give the driver a greater sense of confidence during night-time driving.
