XF SEGURIDAD Y PROTECCIÓN

Disfrute de la gama de sistemas de seguridad que trabajan discretamente para ayudarlo a usted y sus pasajeros a mantenerse protegidos en cada viaje.

PROTECTIVE BODYSHELL

Help to ensure that you and your fellow occupants are protected at all times, XF’s reinforced bodyshell incorporates a range of new-generation high-strength steels to create a vertical safety ring around the cabin.



BOLSAS DE AIRE Y ASIENTOS

Two front airbags, two airbags in the outer seat edges and side curtain airbags above front and rear side windows help provide maximum protection for you and your passengers in the event of a collision.

Sensors in the front seats detect the size and weight of the driver and passenger, allowing seatbelts to mitigate contact with the airbags by activating innovative “soft landing” technology. Meanwhile anti-whiplash technology is designed to instantaneously move the head restraints forward in case of an accident.

SEGURIDAD MÁXIMA

Control the locks remotely from your key fob. The doors will lock automatically when you reach a pre-set speed of your selection. In addition your XF is constantly protected by an alarm and engine immobiliser.

SISTEMA DE MONITOREO DE PRESIÓN DE LLANTAS

Maintaining optimum tyre pressure is vital to your XF’s efficiency and handling. Optional Tyre Pressure Monitoring provides an automatic alert message if pressure drops significantly.

SISTEMA DE DETECCIÓN DE CONTACTO CON EL PEATÓN

Pedestrian Contact Sensing System™ helps protect the people outside your car as well as those inside. If the XF makes contact with a pedestrian, internal sensors instinctively raise the bonnet slightly to help reduce impact.

MANEJO MEJORADO

Your new XF features a number of safety systems designed to improve handling and stability.

These include:

  • Electronic Brake Force distribution
  • Traction Control (through engine and brake intervention)
  • Dynamic Stability Control
  • Cornering Brake Control
  • Engine drag torque control

FRENADO MEJORADO

Helping you retain maximum control in case of the unexpected, XF’s anti-lock brakes vary the pressure during an emergency stop to maintain optimum steering control. You’ll also experience shorter stopping distances and enhanced steering control from the increased braking force applied by Emergency Brake Assist. Meanwhile with your optional Adaptive Cruise Control Intelligent Emergency Braking primes the brakes for optimum power as soon as the radar within senses an imminent collision.

MONITOR DE PUNTOS CIEGOS

Radar sensors take the uncertainty out of changing lanes with a warning signal any time another car enters the driver’s blind spot. An icon in the appropriate external mirror illuminates as soon a vehicle is detected when you choose the optional Blind Spot Monitor.
