SUVs

Leaders in compact SUVs

It shouldn’t come as a surprise that Suzuki invented the compact SUV. Our long history of innovative small car design combined with over 40 years of 4WD experience helped identify the need for vehicles as comfortable in the urban jungle as the sandy beach trail.

Now the category is one of the most popular in the automotive market. But there still isn’t anyone who can beat our experience when it comes to designing and building compact SUVs. Suzuki remain at the forefront of their development, constantly pushing materials, technology, design and engineering.

The result is a range of compact SUVs that deliver comfort and style, performance and practicality, safety and fun – all the positives of an SUV, without the compromise.

Click here to find out why an SUV is right for you and enjoy choosing from our Suzuki range.

Vitara

Reinvented by the brand that started it all, the Suzuki Vitara is the compact SUV designed to give you the freedom to do whatever you like, whenever you want. Go to town customising the Vitara to reflect your personality, stay connected with up-to-date smartphone technology and get adventurous with its ultra-capable off road performance.

  • View the Range

S-Cross

The S-Cross combines stunning European styling, outstanding fuel economy, 5-star safety and a spacious interior into an SUV crossover big enough to fit the most energetic lifestyle. You’ll have no trouble packing the car with everything need to get away and its new generation AWD technology gives you the reassurance to explore in any conditions.

  • View the Range

Grand Vitara

The Suzuki Grand Vitara is a true 4×4 with legendary go-anywhere capability, unbelievable toughness and superior ride comfort. Whether it’s a trip to the snow, the beach for a spot of fishing, or a weekend away camping with the family, the Grand Vitara is the 4WD SUV you can rely on.

  • View the Range

Jimny

A legend for more than forty years, the Suzuki Jimny is the iconic 4×4 off-roader. No road is too difficult, no conditions are too tough, thanks to its selectable low ratio 4WD, unbeatable manoeuvrability and compact dimensions.
