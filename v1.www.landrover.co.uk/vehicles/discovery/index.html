Discovery is always in its element, whether it’s crossing town or taking everyone beyond the city limits for a weekend of adventure.

BOLDER AND MORE DISTINGUISHED THAN EVER

COMFORT AND CONVENIENCE FOR SEVEN ADULTS

Discovery’s stadium seating gives everybody unspoiled views. And a range of infotainment and technologies keep everyone entertained, relaxed and comfortable.

BUILT FOR ADVENTURE

SPECIFICATIONS AT A GLANCE

FUEL EFFICIENCY

35.3mpg

The 3-litre, turbocharged and intercooled SDV6 Diesel is coupled with 8-speed automatic transmission and Intelligent Stop/Start technology.

LUGGAGE SPACE

2,558litres

With second and third row seats stowed in the seven seat option. Whether it’s a weekend away or the trip of a lifetime, Discovery has plenty of room.

TOWING

3,500kg

Discovery’s impressive towing capacity is supported by optional Trailer Stability Assist to keep vehicle and trailer under control.

OBSTACLE CLEARANCE

310mm

Off-road height with Air Suspension and manual lift function. There’s room to manoeuvre on even the toughest terrains.
