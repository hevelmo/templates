Irresistible on the outside

Swift owners fall in love with their cars – and it’s easy to see why. Packed with attitude the Swift is more than just a car – it’s an irresistibly stylish design icon. From its 16 inch alloys (RSX) to its twinkling LED daytime running lamps (RSX), every angle has been designed with a big personality in mind.

Clever on the inside

Powerful, yet economical the Swift is a joy to drive with all the mod-cons you could wish for, 60:40 rear seats, a tall boot area and clever storage spaces. Suzuki’s unparalleled expertise in compact cars has created a cabin optimised for comfort, roominess and a whole lot of fun.

5 star confidence

Driving the Swift is as much about confidence as it is about fun, and with a 5 star ANCAP rating, the Swift is a small car winner. Suzuki’s Total Effective Control Technology (TECT) puts high-strength steel where it’s needed most to increase impact safety. This combined with other cutting-edge safety technology including 7 airbags, ABS, EBD, Brake Assist, and Electronic Stability Programme (ESP®), ensures the Swift is both fun and safe on the road.

Full of fun-ctionality

Functional and fun, the Swift’s every detail is designed to be seen, easily reachable and intuitively helpful. With keyless entry and push-button start (RSX) you’ll never fumble for your keys again. There’s a Sat Nav system to guide you with 2D and 3D maps and Bluetooth connectivity and a USB port, so you can use your smartphone safely while driving. With all these features, you’ll never want to stop!

Outstanding economy

The Swift is so much fun, it can’t keep still. The 1.4L 4-cylinder petrol engine with VVT (Variable Valve Timing) technology, delivers an outstanding power-to-weight ratio with maximum fuel efficiency, or put simply, a lot of fun at minimum cost.

With a design goal of lighter, smaller and cleaner running, the Swift offers enthusiastic performance with outstanding fuel economy of 5.5/100km* and reduced carbon emissions of just 132g/km^.

  • Swift 1.4 Manual 5.5L/100km*
  • Swift 1.4 Auto 6.2L/100km*

*ADR 81/02 results for combined cycle. Fuel consumption will vary due to factors such as vehicle condition, driving style and traffic conditions. ^ based on manual model.

Love the drive

The Swift is big on fun with performance to match. It’s quick to respond, zippy around town and its high performance suspension system is tuned for smooth handling on long stretches.

With a choice of 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmissions, the levers are easy to grip and smooth on the changes. This supermini is born to perform at the touch of your hand.
