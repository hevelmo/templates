EXPLORAR MÁS

1948, SERIES I

Oxford and Cambridge: Far East Expedition

BY Oxford and Cambridge by Land Rover 1955

London, United Kingdom TO Singapore 17116 km

In 1955-1956 two world famous English institutions partnered with a true British icon. The race was officially on for Oxford & Cambridge, complete with two brand new Series I Land Rovers.

Facing 18,000 miles ahead of them, the six courageous university students drove for almost half a year, setting off from London and battling ambitious terrain through Europe, the Middle and Far East, ending their intensive journey in Singapore. This expedition truly promoted the unprecedented abilities of the Series I.

Many had previously considered the teams’ route completely impassable, with the ambitious passage including unmarked desert, jungle and Himalayan passes. The documentation of the treacherous journey was received to great public acclaim, later featuring in a three-part BBC series as well as inspiring a best-selling book.
